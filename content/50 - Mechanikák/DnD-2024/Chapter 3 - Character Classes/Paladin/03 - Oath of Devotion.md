
The Oath of Devotion binds paladins to ideals of honesty, courage, compassion, and justice.  
Those who swear this oath stand as shining examples of righteousness and honor.

---

## Tenets of Devotion

The tenets of this oath define a paladin’s moral code.

- **Honesty.** Don’t lie or cheat. Let your word be your promise.  
- **Courage.** Never fear to act, though caution is wise.  
- **Compassion.** Aid others, protect the weak, and punish those who threaten them.  
- **Honor.** Treat others with fairness and respect.  
- **Duty.** Be responsible for your actions and their consequences.

---

## Oath Spells

You gain oath spells at the paladin levels listed.

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|---------------|--------|
| 3rd | Protection from Evil and Good, Sanctuary |
| 5th | Lesser Restoration, Zone of Truth |
| 9th | Beacon of Hope, Dispel Magic |
| 13th | Freedom of Movement, Guardian of Faith |
| 17th | Commune, Flame Strike |

Oath spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Channel Divinity

When you take this oath at 3rd level, you gain the following Channel Divinity options.

---

### Sacred Weapon

You can imbue your weapon with divine light.

- As an action, you present your holy symbol
- Your weapon shines with radiance
- You add your Charisma modifier to attack rolls with that weapon

The effect lasts for a limited duration.

---

### Turn the Unholy

You can channel divine energy to repel unnatural foes.

- As an action, you present your holy symbol
- Fiends and undead must make a saving throw or be turned

This reflects your dedication to purity and order.

---

## Aura of Devotion

Your devotion protects against corruption.

- You and nearby allies cannot be charmed

---

## Purity of Spirit

You are always under divine protection.

- You are constantly affected by *Protection from Evil and Good*

---

## Holy Nimbus

At 20th level, you radiate divine power.

- You emit bright light
- Enemies take radiant damage
- Fiends and undead suffer additional effects

This feature represents the ultimate expression of your oath.

---

## Oath of Devotion Philosophy

Paladins of this oath serve as moral beacons.

They excel at:
- Defending allies from supernatural threats
- Inspiring trust and courage
- Upholding justice without compromise

Their strength comes from unwavering faith and honor.
