Paladins are holy warriors bound by sacred oaths.  
They combine martial skill with divine magic, standing as champions of justice, devotion, and resolve.

---

## Class Identity

Paladins are defined by:
- Strong frontline combat ability
- Divine spellcasting focused on protection and smiting
- A sacred oath that shapes their powers
- Auras that bolster allies

They excel as defenders, leaders, and decisive strikers.

---

## Primary Ability

**Strength** or **Dexterity** is the paladin’s primary ability score.

- Strength favors heavy armor and melee weapons
- Dexterity favors mobility and finesse

**Charisma** is crucial for:
- Spellcasting
- Class features such as auras and divine abilities

---

## Hit Points

- **Hit Die:** d10 per paladin level

Paladins are durable warriors capable of sustaining heavy punishment.

## Core Class Features

---

## Divine Sense

You can detect evil and powerful supernatural presences.

- As an action, you sense certain creature types or desecrated areas
- Uses are limited and refresh on a Long Rest

---

## Lay on Hands

You can heal wounds through divine touch.

- You have a pool of healing power
- You can restore hit points or cure certain conditions
- The pool refreshes on a Long Rest

---

## Fighting Style

You adopt a combat style that enhances your martial prowess.

- You choose one Fighting Style from the paladin list

---

## Spellcasting

Paladins prepare spells from the paladin spell list.

- Spellcasting ability: Charisma
- Paladin spells emphasize protection, smiting, and support

---

## Divine Smite

You can channel divine power into your weapon strikes.

- When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack
- You can expend a spell slot to deal radiant damage
- The damage increases against certain creature types

Divine Smite defines the paladin’s burst damage potential.

---

## Sacred Oath

At 3rd level, you swear a **Sacred Oath**.

Your oath represents your ideals and grants unique features.

Common oaths include:
- Oath of Devotion
- Oath of Glory
- Oath of Vengeance
- Oath of the Ancients

Each oath is detailed in its own note.

---

## Aura of Protection

Your presence shields allies.

- You and nearby allies gain a bonus to saving throws
- The bonus equals your Charisma modifier

---

## Aura of Courage

Your resolve inspires bravery.

- You and nearby allies are immune to the Frightened condition

---

## Improved Divine Smite

Your weapon strikes are permanently infused with divine power.

- Your melee weapon attacks deal extra radiant damage

---

## Cleansing Touch

You can end harmful magic.

- As an action, you can end spells affecting yourself or others
- Uses are limited per Long Rest

---

## Paladin in the Party

Paladins excel as:
- Frontline defenders
- Burst damage dealers
- Leaders who protect and inspire allies

They are less focused on:
- Stealth
- Long-range spellcasting
