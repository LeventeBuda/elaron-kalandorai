
Soulknives manifest psionic power as blades of pure thought.  
They combine stealth and precision with psychic abilities that require no physical weapons.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Psionic Power

You harness psionic energy represented by **Psionic Energy dice**.

- You have a number of Psionic Energy dice
- The die size increases as you gain rogue levels
- You regain all expended dice on a Long Rest
- You can regain one die by expending a use of Cunning Action

These dice fuel your psionic abilities.

---

## Psychic Blades

You manifest blades of psychic energy.

- You can create a psychic blade in one or both hands
- The blades deal psychic damage
- They leave no physical trace

These blades count as finesse and thrown weapons.

---

## Psionic Strike

You can empower your attacks with psionic force.

- When you hit a target with a psychic blade
- You can expend a Psionic Energy die to deal extra psychic damage

This enhances your burst potential.

---

## Telepathic Whispers

You can communicate mentally.

- You can establish telepathic communication with creatures you can see
- The effect lasts for a limited duration

This feature supports coordination and stealth.

---

## Psychic Veil

You can cloak yourself in psychic invisibility.

- As an action, you become invisible
- The effect lasts for a limited time

This allows repositioning and infiltration.

---

## Rend Mind

Your psionic attacks disrupt enemy minds.

- When you deal psychic damage
- You can impose debilitating mental effects on the target

This adds control to your offense.

---

## Soulknife Philosophy

Soulknives fight with the power of thought.

They excel at:
- Silent combat
- Psychic damage
- Stealth-based infiltration
