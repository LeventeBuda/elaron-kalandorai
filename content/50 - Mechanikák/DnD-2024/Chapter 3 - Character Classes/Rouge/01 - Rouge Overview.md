
Rogues are masters of skill, precision, and opportunity.  
They rely on agility, cunning, and timing to exploit enemy weaknesses and avoid direct confrontation.

---

## Class Identity

Rogues are defined by:
- Exceptional skill proficiency
- Precision damage through Sneak Attack
- High mobility and evasiveness
- Talent for stealth and subterfuge

They excel at scouting, infiltration, and decisive single-target strikes.

---

## Primary Ability

**Dexterity** is the rogue’s primary ability score.

- Used for attacks and damage
- Improves Armor Class
- Enhances Stealth and initiative

**Intelligence**, **Wisdom**, or **Charisma** may be important depending on archetype and role.

---

## Hit Points

- **Hit Die:** d8 per rogue level

Rogues rely on avoidance rather than raw toughness.

## Core Class Features

---

## Expertise

You gain expertise in select skills.

- Choose two skill proficiencies
- Your Proficiency Bonus is doubled for those skills

You gain additional expertise at higher levels.

---

## Sneak Attack

You know how to strike subtly and exploit distraction.

- Once per turn, you deal extra damage when you meet the Sneak Attack conditions
- The damage increases as you gain rogue levels

This feature defines the rogue’s damage output.

---

## Thieves’ Cant

You know a secret language used by rogues.

- You can hide messages in ordinary conversation
- Only other rogues understand its full meaning

---

## Cunning Action

You can take swift tactical actions.

- You can Dash, Disengage, or Hide as a Bonus Action

This gives rogues unmatched combat mobility.

---

## Uncanny Dodge

You can reduce incoming damage.

- When an attacker you can see hits you
- You can use your Reaction to halve the damage

---

## Evasion

You can avoid even dangerous effects.

- On a successful Dexterity saving throw, you take no damage
- On a failure, you take only half damage

---

## Reliable Talent

Your skill checks rarely fail.

- When you make an ability check you’re proficient in
- You treat any roll lower than 10 as a 10

---

## Blindsense

You can detect hidden enemies nearby.

- You are aware of creatures within range even if they are hidden

---

## Slippery Mind

Your mental defenses improve.

- You gain proficiency in Wisdom saving throws

---

## Elusive

You are extremely hard to pin down.

- No attack roll has Advantage against you while you are not incapacitated

---

## Stroke of Luck

You can turn failure into success.

- You can treat a missed attack as a hit
- Or treat a failed ability check as a 20

This feature can be used a limited number of times.

---

## Rogue in the Party

Rogues excel as:
- Scouts and infiltrators
- Precision damage dealers
- Skill specialists

They are less focused on:
- Frontline durability
- Area-of-effect damage
