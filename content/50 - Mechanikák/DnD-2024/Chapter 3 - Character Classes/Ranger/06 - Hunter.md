
Hunters specialize in combating dangerous foes through tactical adaptability.  
They study enemy behavior and develop techniques to counter a wide range of threats.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Hunter’s Prey

You learn specialized techniques for dealing with enemies.

Choose one of the following options.

### Colossus Slayer

- Once per turn, when you hit a creature
- The creature must be below its hit point maximum
- You deal extra damage

---

### Giant Killer

- When a Large or larger creature attacks you
- You can use your Reaction to make a weapon attack against it

---

### Horde Breaker

- Once per turn, when you hit a creature
- You can make an additional attack against another nearby creature

---

## Defensive Tactics

You adopt defensive techniques to survive combat.

Choose one of the following options.

### Escape the Horde

- Opportunity Attacks against you are made with Disadvantage

---

### Multiattack Defense

- When a creature hits you with an attack
- You gain a bonus to AC against subsequent attacks from that creature

---

### Steel Will

- You gain Advantage on saving throws against being frightened

---

## Multiattack

You gain the ability to attack multiple foes.

Choose one of the following options.

### Volley

- As an action, you can make a ranged attack against multiple creatures in an area

---

### Whirlwind Attack

- As an action, you can make melee attacks against all creatures within reach

---

## Superior Hunter’s Defense

You gain powerful defensive reactions.

Choose one of the following options.

### Evasion

- You take no damage on successful Dexterity saving throws

---

### Stand Against the Tide

- When a creature misses you
- You can force it to attack another creature

---

### Uncanny Dodge

- You can use your Reaction to halve the damage from an attack

---

## Hunter Philosophy

Hunters thrive on preparation and adaptability.

They excel at:
- Countering specific enemy types
- Controlling battlefield flow
- Surviving prolonged engagements

Their strength lies in knowing the enemy—and exploiting weaknesses.
