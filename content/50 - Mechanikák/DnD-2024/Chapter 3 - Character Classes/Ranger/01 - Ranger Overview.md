
Rangers are skilled hunters and explorers who thrive on the edges of civilization.  
They combine martial prowess with nature magic to track enemies, survive harsh environments, and strike with precision.

---

## Class Identity

Rangers are defined by:
- Expertise in exploration and survival
- Martial combat with ranged or melee weapons
- Nature-focused spellcasting
- Specialized training against favored foes

They excel at scouting, ambush tactics, and operating independently in dangerous terrain.

---

## Primary Ability

**Dexterity** or **Strength** is the ranger’s primary ability score.

- Dexterity favors ranged combat and stealth
- Strength favors melee combat and heavy weapons

**Wisdom** is important for:
- Spellcasting
- Perception and survival abilities

---

## Hit Points

- **Hit Die:** d10 per ranger level

Rangers are resilient enough to fight on the front lines when needed.

## Core Class Features

---

## Favored Enemy

You have significant experience studying, tracking, and hunting certain enemies.

- Choose one type of favored enemy
- You gain bonuses to tracking and recalling information about them
- Additional favored enemies are gained at higher levels

---

## Natural Explorer

You are particularly familiar with certain environments.

- Choose a favored terrain
- You gain benefits related to travel, foraging, and navigation
- You ignore difficult terrain in your favored environments

---

## Fighting Style

You adopt a combat style reflecting your training.

- Choose one Fighting Style from the ranger list

---

## Spellcasting

Rangers prepare and cast spells drawn from the ranger spell list.

- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom
- Ranger spells emphasize tracking, mobility, and control

---

## Ranger Archetype

At 3rd level, you choose a **Ranger Archetype**.

Your archetype defines your specialized training.

Common archetypes include:
- Beast Master
- Gloom Stalker
- Hunter
- Horizon Walker

Each archetype is detailed in its own note.

---

## Extra Attack

You can attack twice when you take the Attack action on your turn.

---

## Feral Senses

Your awareness borders on supernatural.

- You can perceive invisible creatures within range
- You are rarely caught unaware in combat

---

## Foe Slayer

At 20th level, you become an unparalleled hunter.

- Once per turn, you add your Wisdom modifier to an attack or damage roll against a favored enemy

This feature represents the ultimate predator’s instinct.

---

## Ranger in the Party

Rangers excel as:
- Scouts and trackers
- Ranged damage dealers
- Survival specialists

They are less focused on:
- Heavy armor tanking
- High-level spellcasting
