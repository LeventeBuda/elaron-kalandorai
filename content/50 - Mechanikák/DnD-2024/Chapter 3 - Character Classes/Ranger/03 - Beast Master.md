
Beast Masters form powerful bonds with animal companions.  
Together, ranger and beast fight as a coordinated team, sharing instincts and battlefield awareness.

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Rangerâ€™s Companion

You gain an animal companion that fights alongside you.

- Choose a beast that is Medium or smaller
- The companion acts on your initiative
- You can command the beast to take actions

The companion grows stronger as you gain ranger levels.

---

## Primal Bond

Your bond with your companion deepens.

- You can communicate simple concepts with your beast
- The beast gains additional benefits from your proficiency bonus

This strengthens coordination in combat.

---

## Coordinated Attack

You and your companion strike together.

- When you attack, your companion can make an attack
- This encourages teamwork and positioning

---

## Beast Defense

Your companion becomes more resilient.

- It gains additional hit points
- It gains bonuses to Armor Class and saving throws

This ensures your companion remains viable in combat.

---

## Bestial Fury

Your companion attacks with ferocity.

- When commanded to take the Attack action
- The companion can make multiple attacks

This significantly increases offensive potential.

---

## Share Spells

Your magic can affect your companion.

- When you cast a spell targeting yourself
- The spell can also affect your companion

This feature enhances synergy and survivability.

---

## Beast Master Philosophy

Beast Masters fight through trust and partnership.

They excel at:
- Battlefield coordination
- Flexible positioning
- Combining martial and animal strength

Their power lies in unity with the wild.
