
The Trickery Domain emphasizes deception, misdirection, and stealth.  
Clerics of this domain wield divine power through illusion and cunning, confusing foes and manipulating the battlefield.

---

## Domain Features

You gain the following features when you choose the Trickery Domain.

---

## Domain Spells

You gain domain spells at the cleric levels listed.

| Cleric Level | Spells |
|--------------|--------|
| 1st | Charm Person, Disguise Self |
| 3rd | Mirror Image, Pass without Trace |
| 5th | Blink, Dispel Magic |
| 7th | Dimension Door, Polymorph |
| 9th | Dominate Person, Modify Memory |

Domain spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Blessing of the Trickster

You can bless an ally with deceptive skill.

- As an action, you touch a willing creature
- The target gains Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks

This blessing lasts until you use this feature again.

---

## Channel Divinity: Invoke Duplicity

You can use Channel Divinity to create an illusory duplicate of yourself.

- As an action, you create a perfect illusion of yourself
- You can cast spells as if you were in the duplicateâ€™s space
- The illusion lasts for a limited duration

This feature enables battlefield deception and creative positioning.

---

## Channel Divinity: Cloak of Shadows

You can vanish into invisibility.

- As an action, you become invisible
- The invisibility lasts until the end of your next turn

This feature is useful for repositioning or escape.

---

## Divine Strike

Your attacks are empowered by divine deception.

- Once per turn, you deal extra poison damage on a weapon hit
- The damage increases at higher levels

---

## Improved Duplicity

Your illusion grows more potent.

- You can create more than one duplicate
- You can move the duplicates independently

This greatly expands your tactical options.

---

## Trickery Domain Philosophy

Clerics of the Trickery Domain thrive on confusion and manipulation.

They excel at:
- Stealth and infiltration
- Battlefield control through misdirection
- Social intrigue and deception

Their power lies in staying one step ahead of their enemies.
