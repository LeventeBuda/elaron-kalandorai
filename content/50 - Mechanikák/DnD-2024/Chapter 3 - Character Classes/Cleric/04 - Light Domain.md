
The Light Domain emphasizes illumination, radiance, and the power of burning truth.  
Clerics of this domain wield divine light to scorch enemies, dispel darkness, and protect allies from harm.

---

## Domain Features

You gain the following features when you choose the Light Domain.

---

## Domain Spells

You gain domain spells at the cleric levels listed.

| Cleric Level | Spells |
|--------------|--------|
| 1st | Burning Hands, Faerie Fire |
| 3rd | Flaming Sphere, Scorching Ray |
| 5th | Daylight, Fireball |
| 7th | Guardian of Faith, Wall of Fire |
| 9th | Flame Strike, Scrying |

Domain spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

## Warding Flare

You can interpose divine light to protect yourself.

- When a creature you can see attacks you
- You can use your Reaction to impose Disadvantage on the attack roll

You can use this feature a limited number of times, refreshing on a Long Rest.

---

## Channel Divinity: Radiance of the Dawn

You can use Channel Divinity to unleash a wave of radiant energy.

- As an action, you present your holy symbol
- Magical darkness is dispelled
- Hostile creatures take radiant damage

This feature punishes enemies who rely on darkness or concealment.

---

## Improved Warding Flare

Your protective light extends to others.

- You can use Warding Flare to protect nearby allies
- You can use your Reaction when a creature attacks an ally you can see

---

## Corona of Light

You become a beacon of divine radiance.

- You emit bright light in a wide radius
- Enemies in the light have Disadvantage on saving throws against your fire and radiant spells

This feature dramatically increases your offensive spell effectiveness.

---

## Light Domain Philosophy

Clerics of the Light Domain are champions of revelation.

They excel at:
- Area damage
- Countering darkness and concealment
- Protecting allies through radiant defense

Their magic burns away lies and shadow alike.
