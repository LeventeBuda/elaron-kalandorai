
Druids are spellcasters who draw power from nature itself.  
They revere the natural world and wield primal magic to heal, control the battlefield, and transform into beasts.

---

## Class Identity

Druids are defined by:
- Nature-based spellcasting
- Strong battlefield control and support magic
- A mystical bond with animals and the elements
- The ability to transform through **Wild Shape**

Depending on their circle, druids can fill many roles, from frontline combatant to spell-focused controller.

---

## Primary Ability

**Wisdom** is the druid’s primary ability score.

- Determines spellcasting effectiveness
- Influences spell save DCs and attack rolls
- Supports perception and survival

**Constitution** is important for maintaining concentration and survivability.

---

## Hit Points

- **Hit Die:** d8 per druid level

Druids balance durability and spellcasting similarly to clerics.

## Core Class Features

---

## Spellcasting

Druids prepare spells from the druid spell list.

- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom
- Prepared spells can be changed after a Long Rest
- Many druid spells focus on nature, transformation, and area control

---

## Druidic

You know **Druidic**, the secret language of druids.

- You can speak the language
- You can leave hidden messages in natural forms
- Only other druids can automatically recognize these messages

---

## Wild Shape

You can magically assume the shape of a beast.

- Uses are limited and refresh on a Short or Long Rest
- You gain the beast’s physical statistics
- You retain your alignment, personality, and mental ability scores

Wild Shape is a defining feature of the druid class.

---

## Druid Circle

At 3rd level, you choose a **Druid Circle**.

Your circle represents your philosophical and magical approach to nature.

Common circles include:
- Circle of the Land
- Circle of the Moon
- Circle of the Sea
- Circle of the Stars

Each circle is detailed in its own note.

---

## Timeless Body

Your connection to nature slows your aging.

- For every 10 years that pass, your body ages only 1 year
- You cannot be aged magically

---

## Beast Spells

You can cast spells while in Wild Shape.

- You can cast spells that do not require material components
- You retain the benefits of Wild Shape while casting

---

## Archdruid

At 20th level, you embody the pinnacle of druidic power.

- You can use Wild Shape an unlimited number of times
- You can ignore verbal and somatic components while in Wild Shape

This feature represents complete harmony with nature.

---

## Druid in the Party

Druids excel as:
- Battlefield controllers
- Healers and support casters
- Versatile problem solvers through transformation

They are less focused on:
- Heavy armor combat
- Pure burst damage without preparation
