
Druids of the Circle of the Moon are fierce guardians of the wild who embrace the power of transformation.  
They excel at combat while in beast form, using Wild Shape as a primary tool rather than a utility.

---

## Circle Features

You gain the following features when you choose the Circle of the Moon.

---

## Combat Wild Shape

You can use Wild Shape more effectively in battle.

- You can use Wild Shape as a Bonus Action
- You can expend a spell slot to regain hit points while in beast form

This allows rapid transformation and sustained presence in combat.

---

## Circle Forms

Your connection to the wild allows more powerful transformations.

- You can transform into beasts with higher Challenge Ratings
- The maximum CR increases as you gain druid levels
- You can ignore certain movement restrictions earlier than other druids

This feature defines the Moon druidâ€™s frontline role.

---

## Primal Strike

Your attacks in beast form count as magical.

- Your beast form attacks overcome resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks

---

## Elemental Wild Shape

You can transform into elemental forms.

- You can expend uses of Wild Shape to become an elemental
- Each elemental form offers unique movement and combat abilities

This feature greatly expands your combat versatility.

---

## Thousand Forms

You can cast *Alter Self* at will.

- You can cast the spell without expending a spell slot

This represents mastery over physical transformation.

---

## Circle of the Moon Philosophy

Moon druids are apex shapeshifters.

They excel at:
- Frontline combat
- Damage soaking
- Adaptation through transformation

Their power lies in becoming the wild itself.
