
Druids of the Circle of the Land draw their magic directly from specific natural environments.  
Their power is deeply tied to a chosen terrain, granting expanded spellcasting and endurance.

---

## Circle Features

You gain the following features when you choose the Circle of the Land.

---

## Bonus Cantrip

When you choose this circle:
- You learn one additional druid cantrip of your choice

This cantrip does not count against the number of cantrips you know.

---

## Natural Recovery

You can recover magical energy by communing with nature.

- During a Short Rest, you can recover expended spell slots
- The total level of spell slots recovered is limited
- You cannot recover spell slots of 6th level or higher

This feature reflects the land restoring your magical strength.

---

## Circle Spells

Your connection to a specific land grants additional spells.

Choose a land type. You gain the associated spells at the druid levels listed.  
These spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

## Land’s Stride

You move easily through natural terrain.

- Moving through nonmagical difficult terrain costs you no extra movement
- You have Advantage on saving throws against plants that impede movement

---

## Nature’s Ward

You gain resistance to certain natural hazards.

- You are immune to poison and disease
- You cannot be charmed or frightened by elementals or fey

---

## Nature’s Sanctuary

Creatures of the wild hesitate to attack you.

- Beasts and plants must succeed on a saving throw to attack you
- On a failure, they must choose a different target or waste the attack

---

## Circle of the Land Philosophy

Druids of this circle are guardians of specific places.

They excel at:
- Sustained spellcasting
- Environmental control
- Endurance over long adventuring days

Their magic reflects the steady, persistent power of the land itself.
