
Druids of the Circle of the Sea draw power from oceans, storms, and tides.  
They embody the relentless motion of waves and the destructive force of the deep.

---

## Circle Features

You gain the following features when you choose the Circle of the Sea.

---

## Wrath of the Sea

Your magic echoes the fury of crashing waves.

- When you deal damage with certain druid spells
- You can push or hinder creatures affected by the spell

This feature emphasizes battlefield control through force and motion.

---

## Aquatic Affinity

Your bond with the sea enhances your movement and survival.

- You gain a swimming speed
- You can breathe underwater

This allows you to operate freely in aquatic environments.

---

## Storm-Touched Magic

Your spells carry the power of wind and water.

- You gain additional effects tied to lightning, thunder, or water-based spells
- These effects reinforce the theme of storms and tides

---

## Ocean’s Resilience

The sea hardens those who live within it.

- You gain resistance to certain damage types
- You are better protected against environmental hazards

This feature reflects endurance shaped by harsh waters.

---

## Avatar of the Sea

At higher levels, you become a living embodiment of oceanic power.

- Your presence alters the battlefield
- Waves, wind, and storm answer your call

This feature represents mastery over the sea’s primal forces.

---

## Circle of the Sea Philosophy

Sea druids are relentless and adaptive.

They excel at:
- Area control
- Environmental combat
- Thriving in aquatic and coastal adventures

Their magic mirrors the tide—unstoppable and ever-moving.
