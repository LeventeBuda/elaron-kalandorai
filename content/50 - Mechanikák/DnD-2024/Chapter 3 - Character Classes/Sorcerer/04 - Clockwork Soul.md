
Clockwork Soul sorcerers draw power from cosmic order and absolute law.  
Their magic stabilizes chaos, enforces balance, and rewrites reality into precise, predictable patterns.

---

## Sorcerous Origin Features

You gain the following features when you choose this origin.

---

## Clockwork Spells

You learn additional spells at certain sorcerer levels.

| Sorcerer Level | Spells |
|----------------|--------|
| 1st | Alarm, Protection from Evil and Good |
| 3rd | Aid, Lesser Restoration |
| 5th | Dispel Magic, Protection from Energy |
| 7th | Freedom of Movement, Summon Construct |
| 9th | Greater Restoration, Wall of Force |

These spells are always known and do not count against your spells known.

---

## Restore Balance

You can undo magical advantage or disadvantage.

- When a creature you can see gains Advantage or Disadvantage on a roll
- You can use your Reaction to negate it
- Uses are limited and refresh on a Long Rest

This feature embodies enforced equilibrium.

---

## Bastion of Law

You create a protective ward of order.

- As an action, you expend Sorcery Points
- You create a magical barrier that absorbs damage
- The barrier lasts for a limited duration

This feature converts magic into defense.

---

## Trance of Order

You enter a state of perfect calculation.

- As a Bonus Action, you activate a trance
- Attack rolls against you cannot gain Advantage
- You treat certain dice rolls as fixed results

This feature reflects flawless precision.

---

## Clockwork Cavalcade

You summon spirits of absolute order.

- As an action, you conjure constructs of clockwork force
- They repair, protect, and stabilize the battlefield

This feature represents overwhelming cosmic order.

---

## Clockwork Soul Philosophy

Clockwork Soul sorcerers are agents of balance.

They excel at:
- Defensive control
- Countering chaos and randomness
- Protecting allies through magical order

Their power imposes structure where reality falters.
