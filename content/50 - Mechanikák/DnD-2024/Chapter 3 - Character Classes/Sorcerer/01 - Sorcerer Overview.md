
Sorcerers wield innate magic that flows from within.  
Their power is not learned through study but born of bloodlines, cosmic influence, or raw chaos.

---

## Class Identity

Sorcerers are defined by:
- Innate spellcasting
- Flexible spell shaping through Metamagic
- A limited but potent spell selection
- High burst damage potential

They excel at adapting spells to situations rather than broad versatility.

---

## Primary Ability

**Charisma** is the sorcerer’s primary ability score.

- Determines spell save DCs and spell attack rolls
- Fuels class features tied to magical force of personality

**Constitution** is important for durability and concentration.

---

## Hit Points

- **Hit Die:** d6 per sorcerer level

Sorcerers rely on positioning and magical defense to survive.

## Core Class Features

---

## Spellcasting

Sorcerers cast spells from the sorcerer spell list.

- Spellcasting ability: Charisma
- Sorcerers know a limited number of spells
- Spell selection cannot be easily changed

This makes spell choice critical.

---

## Sorcerous Origin

At 1st level, you choose a **Sorcerous Origin**.

Your origin defines the source of your magic.

Common origins include:
- Aberrant Mind
- Clockwork Soul
- Draconic Bloodline
- Wild Magic

Each origin is detailed in its own note.

---

## Font of Magic

You gain **Sorcery Points**.

- Sorcery Points can be converted into spell slots
- Spell slots can be converted into Sorcery Points

This feature enables flexible resource management.

---

## Metamagic

You gain the ability to alter spells.

- You learn Metamagic options
- You spend Sorcery Points to use them
- Metamagic modifies casting time, range, targets, or effects

This feature defines the sorcerer’s flexibility.

---

## Sorcerous Restoration

You regain magical energy.

- After a Short Rest, you regain Sorcery Points
- This feature ensures sustained spellcasting over the adventuring day

---

## Sorcerer in the Party

Sorcerers excel as:
- Burst damage casters
- Spell specialists
- Flexible battlefield manipulators

They are less focused on:
- Broad utility
- Durability without magic
