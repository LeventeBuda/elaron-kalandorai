
Aberrant Mind sorcerers wield psionic power born from alien influence.  
Their magic reflects warped thoughts, intrusive whispers, and the unsettling presence of the Far Realm.

---

## Sorcerous Origin Features

You gain the following features when you choose this origin.

---

## Psionic Spells

You learn additional spells at certain sorcerer levels.

| Sorcerer Level | Spells |
|----------------|--------|
| 1st | Arms of Hadar, Dissonant Whispers |
| 3rd | Calm Emotions, Detect Thoughts |
| 5th | Hunger of Hadar, Sending |
| 7th | Evard’s Black Tentacles, Summon Aberration |
| 9th | Rary’s Telepathic Bond, Telekinesis |

These spells are always known and do not count against your spells known.

---

## Telepathic Speech

You can form a mental connection with others.

- As a Bonus Action, you choose a creature you can see
- You and the creature can communicate telepathically
- The connection lasts for a limited duration

This feature allows silent coordination and manipulation.

---

## Psionic Sorcery

You can cast psionic spells without verbal or somatic components.

- When you cast a spell from your Psionic Spells list
- You can expend Sorcery Points instead of a spell slot

This makes your magic subtle and difficult to counter.

---

## Psychic Defenses

Your mind resists intrusion.

- You gain resistance to psychic damage
- You have Advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened

This reflects your altered mental structure.

---

## Revelation in Flesh

You can warp your body through psionic mutation.

- As a Bonus Action, you can gain one of several transformative effects
- These effects enhance movement, perception, or survival

This feature emphasizes alien adaptability.

---

## Warping Implosion

You unleash devastating psionic force.

- As an action, you pull creatures toward a point you choose
- Creatures take psychic damage and are displaced

This feature represents catastrophic mental power.

---

## Aberrant Mind Philosophy

Aberrant Mind sorcerers embody alien influence.

They excel at:
- Psychic damage and control
- Silent spellcasting
- Manipulating minds and space

Their power is unsettling, invasive, and deeply unnatural.
