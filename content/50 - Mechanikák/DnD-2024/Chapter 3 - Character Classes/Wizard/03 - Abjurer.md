
Abjurers specialize in protective and warding magic.  
They excel at shielding allies, negating hostile spells, and surviving prolonged magical conflict.

---

## Arcane Tradition Features

You gain the following features when you choose the School of Abjuration.

---

## Abjuration Savant

Your study of abjuration magic is especially efficient.

- The gold and time required to copy abjuration spells into your spellbook are reduced

This encourages specialization in defensive magic.

---

## Arcane Ward

You create a magical ward around yourself.

- When you cast an abjuration spell of 1st level or higher
- You create a ward that absorbs damage
- The ward has its own hit points
- The ward can be restored by casting additional abjuration spells

This feature defines the abjurerâ€™s survivability.

---

## Projected Ward

You can extend your protection to others.

- When a creature you can see takes damage
- You can use your Reaction to absorb the damage with your Arcane Ward

This allows you to protect allies at a distance.

---

## Improved Abjuration

Your counter-magic becomes more potent.

- You add your Proficiency Bonus to ability checks made as part of *Counterspell* and *Dispel Magic*

This greatly increases reliability against powerful magic.

---

## Spell Resistance

You gain heightened resistance to magic.

- You have Advantage on saving throws against spells
- You gain resistance to damage from spells

This feature represents near-immunity to hostile magic.

---

## Abjurer Philosophy

Abjurers are bastions of arcane defense.

They excel at:
- Damage mitigation
- Countering enemy spellcasters
- Protecting the party during prolonged encounters

Their power lies not in destruction, but in denial.
