
Evokers focus on raw magical power and destructive spellcasting.  
They specialize in shaping explosive energy to devastate enemies while protecting allies.

---

## Arcane Tradition Features

You gain the following features when you choose the School of Evocation.

---

## Evocation Savant

Your study of evocation magic is especially efficient.

- The gold and time required to copy evocation spells into your spellbook are reduced

This supports specialization in offensive magic.

---

## Sculpt Spells

You can protect allies from your area spells.

- When you cast an evocation spell that affects other creatures
- You can choose a number of creatures to automatically succeed on their saving throws
- These creatures take no damage on a successful save

This feature defines the evoker’s battlefield control.

---

## Potent Cantrip

Your cantrips become more dangerous.

- Creatures that succeed on saving throws against your damaging cantrips
- Still take half damage

This increases reliability of at-will offense.

---

## Empowered Evocation

You add raw power to your spells.

- You add your Intelligence modifier to one damage roll of any evocation spell

This significantly boosts spell damage.

---

## Overchannel

You can push spells beyond safe limits.

- When you cast an evocation spell of 1st–5th level
- You can deal maximum damage instead of rolling
- Repeated use risks severe backlash

This feature represents ultimate destructive mastery.

---

## Evoker Philosophy

Evokers dominate through overwhelming force.

They excel at:
- Area damage
- Direct magical offense
- Ending fights quickly

Their power is loud, devastating, and unmistakable.
