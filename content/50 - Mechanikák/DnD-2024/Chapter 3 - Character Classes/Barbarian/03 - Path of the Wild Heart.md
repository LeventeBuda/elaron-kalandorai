
The Path of the Wild Heart channels the primal spirits of nature.  
Barbarians who follow this path embody animalistic instincts and draw strength from the untamed world.

---

## Path Features

You gain the following features when you choose this path.

---

## Animal Speaker

Your bond with nature allows you to communicate with beasts.

- You can cast *Speak with Animals* without expending a spell slot
- You can use this feature at will

This ability reflects an instinctive understanding rather than learned magic.

---

## Rage of the Wilds

When you enter Rage, you choose an animal aspect that grants a special benefit for the duration of the Rage.

Each Rage allows you to choose the same aspect or a different one.

### Bear

- You have resistance to all damage except psychic damage

### Eagle

- Other creatures have Disadvantage on Opportunity Attacks against you
- You can Dash as a Bonus Action

### Wolf

- Your allies have Advantage on melee attack rolls against hostile creatures within 5 feet of you

These aspects represent different expressions of primal fury.

---

## Wild Heart Adaptation

As you advance, your bond with the wild deepens.

You gain additional benefits tied to your chosen animal aspects, improving your effectiveness both in and out of combat.

These adaptations reflect learned survival instincts rather than supernatural transformation.

## Wild Heart Philosophy

Barbarians of the Wild Heart are guardians, hunters, and survivors.

They thrive in:
- Wilderness environments
- Mobile combat
- Group tactics that support allies

Their power comes from harmony with nature rather than reckless self-destruction.
