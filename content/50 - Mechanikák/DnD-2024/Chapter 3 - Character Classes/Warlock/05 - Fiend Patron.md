
Fiend warlocks draw power from infernal entities of the Lower Planes.  
Their magic thrives on destruction, temptation, and the harvesting of souls.

---

## Patron Features

You gain the following features from your Fiend patron.

---

## Fiend Spells

You gain additional spells at the warlock levels listed.

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---------------|--------|
| 3rd | Burning Hands, Command |
| 5th | Blindness/Deafness, Scorching Ray |
| 7th | Fireball, Stinking Cloud |
| 9th | Fire Shield, Wall of Fire |

These spells are always known and don’t count against your spells known.

---

## Dark One’s Blessing

Your patron rewards you for slaying enemies.

- When you reduce a hostile creature to 0 hit points
- You gain temporary hit points

This feature sustains you in prolonged combat.

---

## Dark One’s Own Luck

You can call on infernal fortune.

- When you make an ability check or saving throw
- You can add a bonus to the roll
- This feature refreshes after a rest

This grants clutch survivability.

---

## Fiendish Resilience

Your patron hardens your body.

- Choose a damage type
- You gain resistance to that damage type
- You can change the resistance after a rest

This provides adaptive defense.

---

## Hurl Through Hell

You briefly banish an enemy to the Lower Planes.

- When you hit a creature with an attack
- You can send it through hellish torment
- The creature returns taking massive psychic damage

This is a devastating single-target effect.

---

## Fiend Patron Philosophy

Fiend warlocks revel in power gained at a terrible price.

They excel at:
- Fire and destructive magic
- Sustained survivability
- Punishing enemies relentlessly

Their power burns bright—and consumes all it touches.
