
Celestial warlocks are bound to radiant beings of the Upper Planes.  
Their magic blends divine light with arcane power, offering healing, protection, and righteous wrath.

---

## Patron Features

You gain the following features from your Celestial patron.

---

## Celestial Spells

You gain additional spells at the warlock levels listed.

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---------------|--------|
| 3rd | Cure Wounds, Guiding Bolt |
| 5th | Flaming Sphere, Lesser Restoration |
| 7th | Daylight, Revivify |
| 9th | Flame Strike, Greater Restoration |

These spells are always known and don’t count against your spells known.

---

## Healing Light

You gain the ability to heal allies.

- As a Bonus Action, you can heal a creature you can see
- You expend dice granted by your patron
- You can heal at range

This feature provides flexible support.

---

## Radiant Soul

Your patron empowers your radiant and fire magic.

- You gain resistance to radiant damage
- When you deal radiant or fire damage, you add extra damage

This enhances offensive spellcasting.

---

## Celestial Resilience

Your patron shields your allies.

- You and nearby allies gain temporary hit points
- This occurs after a rest

This increases party durability.

---

## Searing Vengeance

You can rise from defeat in a blaze of light.

- When you are reduced to 0 hit points
- You regain hit points and emit radiant energy
- Nearby enemies take radiant damage or are blinded

This feature represents divine intervention.

---

## Celestial Patron Philosophy

Celestial warlocks act as conduits of divine will.

They excel at:
- Support and healing
- Radiant damage
- Sustaining allies through adversity

Their power is compassionate—but formidable.
