
Monks of the Way of the Elements channel elemental forces through disciplined martial arts.  
They harness air, earth, fire, and water, shaping Ki into destructive and controlling techniques.

---

## Tradition Features

You gain the following features when you choose this tradition.

---

## Disciple of the Elements

You learn elemental disciplines that allow you to manipulate the elements.

- You gain access to elemental disciplines fueled by Ki
- Each discipline requires a certain monk level and Ki cost
- Disciplines replicate elemental spell-like effects

These techniques are expressions of focused Ki rather than spellcasting.

---

## Elemental Disciplines

Elemental disciplines allow you to:
- Create blasts of fire or cold
- Manipulate earth and stone
- Control wind and water
- Enhance movement and battlefield control

Each discipline has:
- A Ki cost
- A required monk level
- A specific elemental theme

---

## Elemental Attunement

You gain minor elemental control.

- You can manipulate small elemental effects
- These effects are cosmetic or utility-focused

This feature represents basic harmony with the elements.

---

## Improved Elemental Techniques

As you gain levels:
- You learn additional elemental disciplines
- Existing disciplines become more powerful
- You gain access to stronger elemental effects

This reflects growing mastery over the elements.

---

## Elemental Mastery

At higher levels, your Ki can unleash devastating elemental force.

- You can use disciplines that rival powerful spells
- Your elemental attacks shape large areas of the battlefield

This feature emphasizes spectacle and area control.

---

## Way of the Elements Philosophy

Monks of this tradition balance martial discipline with raw elemental power.

They excel at:
- Area damage
- Battlefield manipulation
- Thematic, elemental combat

Their strength comes from harmony between body, mind, and the natural forces.
