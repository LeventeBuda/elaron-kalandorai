
Eldritch Knights blend martial mastery with arcane magic.  
They enhance weapon combat with spells, wards, and battlefield control.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Spellcasting

You learn to cast wizard spells.

- Spellcasting ability: Intelligence
- You know a limited number of spells from the wizard spell list
- Most of your spells must be from the abjuration or evocation schools

You gain spell slots and spells known as shown on the Eldritch Knight table.

---

## Weapon Bond

You forge a magical bond with your weapon.

- You can bond with up to two weapons
- You can summon a bonded weapon to your hand as a Bonus Action
- You cannot be disarmed of a bonded weapon unless you are incapacitated

This feature ensures constant access to your weapons.

---

## War Magic

You learn to blend spellcasting and weapon attacks.

- When you use your Action to cast a cantrip
- You can make one weapon attack as a Bonus Action

This feature encourages spell-and-strike tactics.

---

## Eldritch Strike

Your weapon attacks disrupt enemy defenses.

- When you hit a creature with a weapon attack
- The creature has Disadvantage on the next saving throw it makes against a spell you cast before the end of your next turn

This feature improves spell reliability in combat.

---

## Arcane Charge

You teleport through arcane energy.

- When you use Action Surge
- You can teleport a short distance before or after the additional action

This enhances mobility and positioning.

---

## Improved War Magic

Your spell-and-blade techniques reach their peak.

- When you use your Action to cast a spell
- You can make one weapon attack as a Bonus Action

This expands War Magic to higher-level spells.

---

## Eldritch Knight Philosophy

Eldritch Knights excel at versatility.

They thrive when:
- Magic and martial combat must be combined
- Defensive spells are crucial
- Tactical flexibility is rewarded

Their strength lies in discipline guided by arcane study.
