
Champions focus on raw physical excellence and relentless offense.  
They rely on athletic prowess and improved combat fundamentals rather than complex techniques or magic.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Improved Critical

Your attacks strike true more often.

- Your weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20

This feature increases your damage consistency over time.

---

## Remarkable Athlete

Your physical capabilities surpass normal limits.

- You add half your Proficiency Bonus (rounded up) to Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution checks you aren’t proficient in
- Your jumping distance increases

This feature reflects superior athletic conditioning.

---

## Additional Fighting Style

You refine your combat approach.

- You gain an additional Fighting Style of your choice

This allows greater customization and flexibility in combat.

---

## Superior Critical

Your attacks become even deadlier.

- Your weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 18–20

This further improves your damage potential.

---

## Survivor

You can recover from injuries with remarkable speed.

- At the start of each of your turns, you regain hit points if you are below half your hit point maximum
- You must be conscious to benefit from this feature

This feature grants exceptional endurance in prolonged fights.

---

## Champion Philosophy

Champions excel through simplicity and strength.

They thrive when:
- Combat is direct and prolonged
- Reliability matters more than complexity
- Physical prowess defines success

Their power lies in mastering the fundamentals of battle.
