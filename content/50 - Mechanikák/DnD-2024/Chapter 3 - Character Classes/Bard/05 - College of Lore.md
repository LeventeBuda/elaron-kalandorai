
Bards of the College of Lore are scholars, storytellers, and collectors of forgotten knowledge.  
They use wit, words, and magic to outthink foes and support allies.

---

## College Features

You gain the following features when you join the College of Lore.

---

## Bonus Proficiencies

When you choose this college:
- You gain proficiency in three skills of your choice

This reflects broad academic and practical knowledge.

---

## Cutting Words

You learn how to use wit to distract and undermine enemies.

- When a creature you can see makes an attack roll, ability check, or damage roll
- You can use your Reaction and expend one Bardic Inspiration die
- Roll the die and subtract the result from the creature’s roll

You can choose to use this feature after the creature rolls, but before the outcome is known.

---

## Additional Magical Secrets

Your pursuit of knowledge expands your spellcasting options.

At higher levels:
- You learn additional spells from any class’s spell list
- These spells count as bard spells for you

This feature enhances versatility beyond the normal bard spell list.

---

## Peerless Skill

Your mastery allows you to push beyond normal limits.

- When you make an ability check
- You can expend one Bardic Inspiration die
- Add the result to your roll

You can use this feature after rolling the d20, but before the outcome is determined.

---

## College of Lore Philosophy

Lore bards excel through knowledge and preparation.

They thrive when:
- Information and planning matter
- Skill challenges are frequent
- Creative spell use is rewarded

Their strength lies in adaptability and insight rather than raw force.
