
Bards of the College of Glamour wield magic born of beauty, presence, and otherworldly allure.  
Their performances captivate hearts and command attention, turning admiration into power.

---

## College Features

You gain the following features when you join the College of Glamour.

---

## Mantle of Inspiration

Your performance fills allies with confidence and grace.

- As a Bonus Action, you can expend a use of Bardic Inspiration
- Choose creatures you can see within range
- Each target gains temporary hit points
- Each target can immediately use its Reaction to move without provoking Opportunity Attacks

This feature allows rapid repositioning and protection at the start of a fight.

---

## Enthralling Performance

Your presence can entrance an audience.

- After performing for at least 1 minute
- Choose humanoids who watched and listened
- Each target must succeed on a saving throw or become Charmed by you

The charm lasts for a limited duration and ends if the creature is harmed.

---

## Mantle of Majesty

You can cloak yourself in supernatural authority.

- As a Bonus Action, you can assume a majestic presence
- For the duration, you can cast *Command* without expending spell slots
- Creatures charmed by you automatically fail their saving throws against the spell

This feature turns social dominance into battlefield control.

---

## Unbreakable Majesty

Your beauty and authority make it difficult to strike you.

- Creatures that try to attack you must first succeed on a saving throw
- On a failure, the attack is wasted

This effect reflects awe, hesitation, or overwhelming admiration.

---

## College of Glamour Philosophy

Glamour bards dominate through presence rather than force.

They excel at:
- Crowd control
- Battlefield positioning
- Social encounters and influence

Their magic thrives where attention and emotion matter most.
