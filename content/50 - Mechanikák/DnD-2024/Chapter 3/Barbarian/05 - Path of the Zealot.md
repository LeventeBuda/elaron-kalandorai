
The Path of the Zealot is fueled by divine fury.  
Barbarians who follow this path are chosen instruments of a higher power, driven by faith, destiny, or sacred purpose.

Their rage is both wrath and devotion.

---

## Path Features

You gain the following features when you choose this path.

---

## Divine Fury

Your rage is infused with divine power.

While you are raging:
- The first creature you hit on each of your turns takes extra damage
- The extra damage is radiant or necrotic (your choice)

This damage reflects the will of the divine force that empowers you.

---

## Warrior of the Gods

Your soul is marked for divine favor.

- You are easier to restore to life
- Spells that return you from death require fewer material resources

This feature represents your destiny being protected by higher powers.

---

## Fanatical Focus

Your faith steels your resolve.

- If you fail a saving throw while raging, you can reroll it
- You must use the new roll

This ability represents unwavering conviction in the face of overwhelming force.

---

## Zealous Presence

Your divine fervor inspires others.

As a bonus action:
- You can grant Advantage to yourself and nearby allies
- The effect lasts for a limited time

This feature turns your rage into a rallying force for the group.

---

## Rage Beyond Death

Your fury refuses to let you fall.

While raging:
- Dropping to 0 hit points does not knock you unconscious
- You can continue fighting despite mortal wounds

You still die if your rage ends while you remain at 0 hit points.

This feature represents sheer divine defiance of death itself.

---

## Zealot Philosophy

Zealots are avatars of belief.

They excel when:
- Facing overwhelming odds
- Battling undead or fiends
- Holding the line when others would fall
