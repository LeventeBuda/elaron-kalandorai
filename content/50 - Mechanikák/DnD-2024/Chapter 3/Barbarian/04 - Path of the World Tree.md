
The Path of the World Tree binds a barbarian’s rage to the primal force that connects all realms of existence.  
Followers of this path draw strength, vitality, and reach from the roots and branches of the World Tree itself.

---

## Path Features

You gain the following features when you choose this path.

---

## Vitality of the Tree

Your rage carries restorative power.

While you are raging:
- You or an ally you can reach can regain hit points
- This healing reflects the life-giving sap of the World Tree

This feature allows you to support allies while remaining in the heart of battle.

---

## Branches of Reach

The power of the World Tree extends your grasp.

- Your melee weapon attacks can affect creatures beyond your normal reach
- This extension represents spectral branches or roots striking your foes

This feature improves battlefield control and positioning.

---

## Grasping Roots

Your rage can manifest binding roots.

When you hit a creature:
- You can attempt to restrain or hinder it
- The effect represents roots erupting from the ground or spectral growths binding the target

This ability emphasizes control over pure damage.

---

## Guardian of the Tree

Your connection to the World Tree strengthens your protective instincts.

You gain features that:
- Reduce harm to allies
- Punish enemies who threaten those under your protection

You act as both warrior and warden.

---

## Avatar of the World Tree

At higher levels, your bond reaches its peak.

- Your rage fully channels the World Tree’s power
- Your presence on the battlefield reshapes space, reach, and resilience

This feature represents a near-mythic union with the World Tree.

---

## World Tree Philosophy

Barbarians of the World Tree are defenders and anchors.

They excel at:
- Protecting allies
- Controlling enemy movement
- Sustaining the party through prolonged conflict

Their rage is not reckless—it is enduring, rooted, and inevitable.
