
Druids of the Circle of the Stars draw power from the constellations and the cosmic forces they represent.  
They read omens in the night sky and channel stellar magic to guide, heal, and destroy.

---

## Circle Features

You gain the following features when you choose the Circle of the Stars.

---

## Star Map

You create a magical star map that serves as a focus for your power.

- The star map can take various forms, such as a scroll, tablet, or crystal
- It functions as a spellcasting focus for your druid spells
- You gain access to additional spells through this map

This feature represents your study of the heavens.

---

## Starry Form

You can channel stellar power into a radiant form.

- As a Bonus Action, you expend a use of Wild Shape
- You assume a starry form that sheds light
- While in this form, you gain benefits based on a chosen constellation

### Archer

- You can make a radiant ranged attack as a Bonus Action

### Chalice

- When you cast a spell that restores hit points, additional healing occurs

### Dragon

- You gain bonuses to concentration checks
- Your mind aligns with cosmic order

---

## Cosmic Omen

You learn to read signs in the stars.

- After a Long Rest, you roll to determine a beneficial or harmful omen
- You can apply the omen to influence rolls during the day

This feature reflects foresight guided by celestial patterns.

---

## Twinkling Constellations

Your starry forms grow more powerful.

- The effects of your chosen constellation improve
- You gain greater flexibility while in Starry Form

---

## Full of Stars

Your body becomes suffused with starlight.

- You gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage
- Your physical form appears partially luminous

This feature represents near-complete alignment with the cosmos.

---

## Circle of the Stars Philosophy

Star druids are mystics and seers.

They excel at:
- Support and healing
- Ranged damage
- Maintaining concentration on powerful spells

Their magic bridges the natural world and the infinite sky above.
