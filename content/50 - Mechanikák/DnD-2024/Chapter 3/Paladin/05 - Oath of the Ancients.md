
The Oath of the Ancients binds paladins to the preservation of life, light, and joy.  
These paladins stand against darkness, nurturing hope and defending the natural world.

---

## Tenets of the Ancients

The tenets of this oath emphasize resilience, kindness, and the enduring light of the world.

- **Kindle the Light.** Through mercy and courage, keep hope alive.  
- **Shelter the Light.** Protect beauty, life, and those who cannot protect themselves.  
- **Preserve Your Own Light.** Delight in song, laughter, and the simple joys of life.  
- **Be the Light.** Act as a beacon against despair and corruption.

---

## Oath Spells

You gain oath spells at the paladin levels listed.

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|---------------|--------|
| 3rd | Ensnaring Strike, Speak with Animals |
| 5th | Moonbeam, Misty Step |
| 9th | Plant Growth, Protection from Energy |
| 13th | Ice Storm, Stoneskin |
| 17th | Commune with Nature, Tree Stride |

Oath spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Channel Divinity

When you take this oath at 3rd level, you gain the following Channel Divinity options.

---

### Nature’s Wrath

You can restrain foes with spectral vines.

- As an action, you invoke your Channel Divinity
- Creatures you can see must succeed on a saving throw or be restrained

This represents nature rising to defend the light.

---

### Turn the Faithless

You can repel unnatural creatures.

- As an action, you present your holy symbol
- Fey and fiends must make a saving throw or be turned

This protects the natural order from corruption.

---

## Aura of Warding

Your presence shields against harmful magic.

- You and nearby allies gain resistance to damage from spells

This aura embodies ancient protective magic.

---

## Undying Sentinel

Your vitality resists mortal limits.

- When you drop to 0 hit points, you can remain standing
- You also suffer fewer effects from old age

This reflects life’s refusal to yield to darkness.

---

## Elder Champion

At 20th level, you become a legendary guardian of life.

- You regain hit points each turn
- Your spells are cast more swiftly
- Enemies within your aura suffer disadvantages

This feature represents the full blessing of ancient powers.

---

## Oath of the Ancients Philosophy

Paladins of this oath are guardians of hope.

They excel at:
- Defending allies against magic
- Sustaining long battles
- Standing firm against despair and corruption

Their strength comes from joy, life, and enduring light.
