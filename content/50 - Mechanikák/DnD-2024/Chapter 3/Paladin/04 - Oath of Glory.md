
The Oath of Glory binds paladins to the pursuit of greatness through heroic deeds.  
Those who swear this oath seek to achieve legendary status, inspiring others through strength, courage, and bold action.

---

## Tenets of Glory

The tenets of this oath call paladins to heroic excellence.

- **Actions over Words.** Strive to be known by glorious deeds, not promises.  
- **Challenges Are Tests.** Face hardships as opportunities to prove worth.  
- **Hone the Body.** Physical perfection reflects inner strength.  
- **Discipline the Soul.** True glory comes from mastery of self.

---

## Oath Spells

You gain oath spells at the paladin levels listed.

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|---------------|--------|
| 3rd | Guiding Bolt, Heroism |
| 5th | Enhance Ability, Magic Weapon |
| 9th | Haste, Protection from Energy |
| 13th | Compulsion, Freedom of Movement |
| 17th | Commune, Flame Strike |

Oath spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Channel Divinity

When you take this oath at 3rd level, you gain the following Channel Divinity options.

---

### Peerless Athlete

You channel divine power to enhance physical prowess.

- As a Bonus Action, you gain Advantage on Strength and Dexterity checks
- Your carrying capacity, jump distance, and athletic feats improve

This feature reflects heroic physical excellence.

---

### Inspiring Smite

You turn divine punishment into inspiration.

- Immediately after you deal damage with Divine Smite
- You can grant temporary hit points to nearby allies

This encourages aggressive, rallying combat.

---

## Aura of Alacrity

Your presence inspires swift action.

- You and nearby allies gain increased movement speed

This aura emphasizes momentum and mobility.

## Living Legend

At 20th level, you embody heroic myth.

- You gain Advantage on Charisma checks
- Your attacks and saving throws gain additional benefits
- You radiate an aura of awe and legend

This feature represents the pinnacle of mortal glory.

---

## Oath of Glory Philosophy

Paladins of this oath strive for greatness.

They excel at:
- Leading charges
- Athletic combat
- Inspiring allies through action

Their strength lies in proving that mortals can achieve legend.
