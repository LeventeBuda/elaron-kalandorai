
The Oath of Vengeance binds paladins to the relentless pursuit of justice.  
Those who swear this oath focus on punishing evildoers, stopping great threats, and delivering righteous retribution.

---

## Tenets of Vengeance

The tenets of this oath emphasize resolve and ruthless focus.

- **Fight the Greater Evil.** Prioritize the most dangerous foes.  
- **No Mercy for the Wicked.** Do not relent against those who spread evil.  
- **By Any Means Necessary.** If the path is just, use whatever tools are required.  
- **Restitution.** Help those harmed by the enemy’s actions.

---

## Oath Spells

You gain oath spells at the paladin levels listed.

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|---------------|--------|
| 3rd | Bane, Hunter’s Mark |
| 5th | Hold Person, Misty Step |
| 9th | Haste, Protection from Energy |
| 13th | Banishment, Dimension Door |
| 17th | Hold Monster, Scrying |

Oath spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Channel Divinity

When you take this oath at 3rd level, you gain the following Channel Divinity options.

---

### Abjure Enemy

You can channel divine wrath to halt your foe.

- As an action, you present your holy symbol
- A creature you can see must succeed on a saving throw or be frightened and restrained

This feature allows you to lock down priority targets.

---

### Vow of Enmity

You swear a personal oath against a single foe.

- As a Bonus Action, you designate one creature
- You gain Advantage on attack rolls against that creature

This feature defines the vengeance paladin’s single-target focus.

---

## Relentless Avenger

You pursue fleeing enemies without hesitation.

- When you hit a creature with an Opportunity Attack
- You can move as part of the same reaction

This ensures enemies cannot easily escape your judgment.

---

## Soul of Vengeance

Your sworn foe cannot escape retribution.

- When a creature affected by your Vow of Enmity makes an attack
- You can use your Reaction to make a weapon attack against it

This maintains pressure even outside your turn.

---

## Avenging Angel

At 20th level, you assume a terrifying divine form.

- You gain a flying speed
- Enemies become frightened of you
- You radiate an aura of dread

This feature represents ultimate divine vengeance made manifest.

---

## Oath of Vengeance Philosophy

Paladins of this oath are relentless hunters.

They excel at:
- Single-target elimination
- Pursuit and mobility
- Shutting down major threats

Their power comes from unwavering focus and righteous fury.
