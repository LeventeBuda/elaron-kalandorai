
Monks of the Way of Shadow embrace stealth, silence, and deception.  
They train to operate unseen, striking from darkness and vanishing before enemies can respond.

---

## Tradition Features

You gain the following features when you choose this tradition.

---

## Shadow Arts

You learn to use Ki to mimic shadow magic.

- You can spend Ki to cast certain spells
- These spells emphasize stealth, misdirection, and darkness
- You gain access to spells such as *Darkness*, *Darkvision*, *Pass without Trace*, and *Silence*

These spells are cast without material components.

---

## Shadow Step

You can move through shadows with supernatural speed.

- While in dim light or darkness
- You can teleport a short distance to another area of dim light or darkness
- You gain Advantage on the first melee attack you make before the end of the turn

This feature enables rapid repositioning and ambush tactics.

---

## Cloak of Shadows

You can vanish into darkness.

- As an action, you become invisible while you remain in dim light or darkness
- The invisibility ends if you attack, cast a spell, or enter bright light

This feature reinforces infiltration and escape.

---

## Opportunist

You strike when enemies are distracted.

- When a creature within reach is hit by another creature
- You can use your Reaction to make a melee attack against it

This allows you to capitalize on openings in combat.

---

## Way of Shadow Philosophy

Shadow monks excel at precision and control.

They thrive when:
- Stealth and positioning matter
- Enemies rely on vision or formation
- Quick elimination of key targets is required

Their strength lies in patience, timing, and silence.
