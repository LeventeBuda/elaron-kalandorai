
Monks of the Way of the Open Hand master the fundamental techniques of martial arts.  
They focus on control, precision, and physical perfection rather than weapons or mystical forces.

---

## Tradition Features

You gain the following features when you choose this tradition.

---

## Open Hand Technique

You manipulate enemies through precise strikes.

- When you hit a creature with Flurry of Blows
- You can impose one of several effects on the target:
  - Knock the target prone
  - Push the target away
  - Prevent the target from taking reactions

This feature emphasizes battlefield control.

---

## Wholeness of Body

You can heal yourself through focused meditation.

- As an action, you regain hit points
- This feature has limited uses and refreshes on a Long Rest

This reflects mastery over body and Ki.

---

## Tranquility

Your presence radiates peace.

- After finishing a Long Rest, you gain the effects of *Sanctuary*
- The effect lasts until you attack or cast a harmful spell

This feature enables nonviolent solutions and positioning.

---

## Quivering Palm

You learn a devastating technique that disrupts life energy.

- When you hit a creature with an unarmed strike
- You can set up lethal vibrations within its body
- The effect can be triggered later to deal massive damage or kill outright

This feature represents the pinnacle of martial precision.

---

## Way of the Open Hand Philosophy

Open Hand monks embody discipline and mastery.

They excel at:
- Single-target control
- Tactical repositioning
- Clean, efficient combat

Their power lies in perfect technique rather than flashy effects.
