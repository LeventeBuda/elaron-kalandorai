
Wizards are supreme scholars of arcane magic.  
Through study, experimentation, and discipline, they unlock the deepest secrets of spellcasting.

---

## Class Identity

Wizards are defined by:
- Extensive spell knowledge
- Preparation-based spellcasting
- Arcane specialization through schools of magic
- Exceptional versatility and utility

They excel at problem-solving, battlefield control, and magical research.

---

## Primary Ability

**Intelligence** is the wizard’s primary ability score.

- Determines spell save DCs and spell attack rolls
- Governs spell preparation and arcane knowledge

**Constitution** is important for concentration and survival.

---

## Hit Points

- **Hit Die:** d6 per wizard level

Wizards rely on magic and positioning rather than durability.

## Core Class Features

---

## Spellcasting

Wizards cast spells from the wizard spell list.

- Spellcasting ability: Intelligence
- Wizards prepare spells from their spellbook
- Prepared spells can be changed after a Long Rest

Spell preparation allows exceptional adaptability.

---

## Spellbook

Your spellbook contains the spells you know.

- You begin with a set of wizard spells
- You can add new spells by copying them from scrolls or other spellbooks
- Adding spells requires time and resources

The spellbook defines a wizard’s long-term power.

---

## Ritual Casting

You can cast certain spells as rituals.

- Ritual spells do not consume spell slots
- Casting a ritual takes additional time
- The spell must be in your spellbook

This enables powerful utility outside combat.

---

## Arcane Recovery

You can recover magical energy.

- After a Short Rest, you regain expended spell slots
- The total level of slots recovered is limited

This allows sustained casting over an adventuring day.

---

## School of Magic

At 2nd level, you choose a **School of Magic**.

Available schools include:
- Abjuration
- Divination
- Evocation
- Illusion

Each school grants unique features and advantages.

---

## Spell Mastery

You achieve effortless command over basic spells.

- Choose one 1st-level spell and one 2nd-level spell
- You can cast them without expending spell slots

This reflects supreme arcane efficiency.

---

## Signature Spells

You permanently master select spells.

- Choose two 3rd-level wizard spells
- You can cast them at higher levels with ease

This represents ultimate magical expertise.

---

## Wizard in the Party

Wizards excel as:
- Battlefield controllers
- Magical problem-solvers
- Arcane researchers

They are less focused on:
- Durability
- Spontaneous casting
