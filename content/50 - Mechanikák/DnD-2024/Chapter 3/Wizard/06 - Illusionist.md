
Illusionists manipulate perception and reality through deception.  
Their magic bends the senses, conceals truth, and turns belief into a weapon.

---

## Arcane Tradition Features

You gain the following features when you choose the School of Illusion.

---

## Illusion Savant

Your study of illusion magic is especially efficient.

- The gold and time required to copy illusion spells into your spellbook are reduced

This encourages mastery of deceptive magic.

---

## Improved Minor Illusion

Your *Minor Illusion* cantrip becomes more versatile.

- You can create both a sound and an image with a single casting

This greatly enhances creative applications.

---

## Malleable Illusions

You can reshape your illusions.

- When you cast an illusion spell with a duration
- You can alter its nature as an action

This allows dynamic manipulation during encounters.

---

## Illusory Self

You can momentarily avoid harm.

- When you are hit by an attack
- You can cause the attack to miss by interposing an illusion
- This feature can be used a limited number of times

This provides powerful defensive utility.

---

## Illusory Reality

You make illusions partially real.

- You can choose an object created by an illusion spell
- That object becomes real for a short time

This feature represents ultimate mastery of illusion.

---

## Illusionist Philosophy

Illusionists win through misdirection.

They excel at:
- Battlefield deception
- Psychological warfare
- Creative problem-solving

Their power lies in convincing the world of a lie.
