
Diviners peer into the threads of fate and probability.  
They manipulate outcomes, foresee danger, and bend chance to their advantage.

---

## Arcane Tradition Features

You gain the following features when you choose the School of Divination.

---

## Divination Savant

Your study of divination magic is especially efficient.

- The gold and time required to copy divination spells into your spellbook are reduced

This encourages mastery of foresight and information magic.

---

## Portent

You glimpse the future and record omens.

- After a Long Rest, you roll a set of d20s
- You can replace attack rolls, saving throws, or ability checks with these rolls
- You must choose to do so before the roll is made

This feature defines the divinerâ€™s control over fate.

---

## Expert Divination

Your foresight fuels further magic.

- When you cast a divination spell of 2nd level or higher
- You regain a lower-level spell slot

This rewards frequent use of divination spells.

---

## The Third Eye

You gain enhanced magical perception.

- As an action, you can gain one of several sensory benefits
- Options include improved vision, awareness of invisibility, or ethereal sight

This feature enhances battlefield awareness and exploration.

---

## Greater Portent

Your visions deepen.

- You roll additional Portent dice
- You gain more opportunities to alter fate

This amplifies your influence over critical moments.

---

## Diviner Philosophy

Diviners shape reality through knowledge.

They excel at:
- Controlling outcomes
- Preventing disaster
- Strategic planning and foresight

Their greatest weapon is certainty in an uncertain world.
