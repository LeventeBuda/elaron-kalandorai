
Warlocks gain magic through pacts with powerful otherworldly patrons.  
Their spellcasting is limited but potent, fueled by short rests and enhanced by eldritch secrets.

---

## Class Identity

Warlocks are defined by:
- A supernatural patron
- Pact Magic with few but powerful spell slots
- Eldritch Invocations that customize abilities
- Pact Boons that shape playstyle

They excel at sustained magical pressure and unique build flexibility.

---

## Primary Ability

**Charisma** is the warlock’s primary ability score.

- Determines spell save DCs and spell attack rolls
- Fuels many class features

**Constitution** is important for concentration and survivability.

---

## Core Class Features

---

## Pact Magic

You cast spells through a pact with your patron.

- Spellcasting ability: Charisma
- You have a small number of spell slots
- All spell slots are the same level
- Spell slots refresh on a Short or Long Rest

This allows frequent use of high-level magic.

---

## Otherworldly Patron

At 1st level, you choose a patron that grants your power.

Available patrons include:
- Archfey
- Celestial
- Fiend
- Great Old One

Each patron is detailed in its own note.

---

## Eldritch Invocations

You learn magical enhancements known as **Eldritch Invocations**.

- You learn invocations at 2nd level
- Some invocations have prerequisites
- You can change invocations when you gain a level

### Common Invocation Themes
- Enhancing *eldritch blast*
- Improving defenses
- Granting at-will spells
- Expanding pact abilities

Eldritch Invocations define the warlock’s customization.

---

## Pact Boon

At 3rd level, you choose a **Pact Boon**.

### Pact of the Blade
- You conjure or bind a weapon
- You can use Charisma for weapon attacks
- Enables melee-focused warlocks

### Pact of the Chain
- You gain a powerful familiar
- The familiar gains enhanced abilities

### Pact of the Tome
- You gain a Book of Shadows
- You learn additional cantrips
- Grants ritual casting options

Pact Boons strongly influence playstyle.

---

## Mystic Arcanum

You gain access to powerful spells beyond Pact Magic.

- You gain one spell of 6th level or higher
- You can cast it once per Long Rest
- Each Arcanum is chosen separately

These spells represent rare, supreme knowledge.

---

## Eldritch Master

At 20th level, you can draw directly on your patron.

- After 1 minute of supplication
- You regain all expended Pact Magic spell slots

This feature ensures continued magical dominance.

---

## Warlock in the Party

Warlocks excel as:
- Consistent magical damage dealers
- Short-rest-based casters
- Highly customizable specialists

They are less focused on:
- Large spell lists
- Traditional prepared casting
