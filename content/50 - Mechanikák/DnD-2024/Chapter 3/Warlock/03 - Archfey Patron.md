
Archfey warlocks draw power from enigmatic lords of the Feywild.  
Their magic bends emotions, perception, and movement, favoring charm, illusion, and misdirection.

---

## Patron Features

You gain the following features from your Archfey patron.

---

## Archfey Spells

You gain additional spells at the warlock levels listed.

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---------------|--------|
| 3rd | Calm Emotions, Faerie Fire, Misty Step |
| 5th | Blink, Plant Growth |
| 7th | Dominate Beast, Greater Invisibility |
| 9th | Dominate Person, Seeming |

These spells are always known and don’t count against your spells known.

---

## Fey Presence

You can unleash beguiling fey magic.

- As an action, you force creatures in an area to make a saving throw
- On a failure, they are charmed or frightened by you
- The effect lasts for a short duration

This feature enables battlefield control through emotion.

---

## Misty Escape

You can vanish in a swirl of fey magic.

- When you take damage, you can use your Reaction
- You turn invisible and teleport a short distance
- The invisibility ends after a brief time or when you attack

This provides powerful defensive repositioning.

---

## Beguiling Defenses

Your patron wards your mind.

- You gain immunity to being charmed
- When a creature attempts to charm you, you can turn the effect back on it

This punishes enemies who rely on enchantment.

---

## Dark Delirium

You can trap a creature in an illusory nightmare.

- As an action, choose a creature you can see
- The creature is isolated in a fey illusion
- While affected, it is charmed or frightened by you

This feature removes a major threat from the fight temporarily.

---

## Archfey Patron Philosophy

Archfey warlocks thrive on subtlety and control.

They excel at:
- Enchantment and illusion
- Mobility and escape
- Manipulating emotions and perception

Their power is whimsical, beautiful—and dangerous.
