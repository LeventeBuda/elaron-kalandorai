
Gloom Stalkers are masters of ambush who thrive in darkness.  
They specialize in striking first, moving unseen, and overwhelming enemies before a fight truly begins.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Gloom Stalker Magic

You learn additional spells at certain ranger levels.

| Ranger Level | Spells |
|-------------|--------|
| 3rd | Disguise Self |
| 5th | Rope Trick |
| 9th | Fear |
| 13th | Greater Invisibility |
| 17th | Seeming |

These spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Dread Ambusher

You master the art of the opening strike.

- You gain a bonus to initiative
- On the first turn of combat, your speed increases
- You can make an additional attack that deals extra damage

This feature defines the Gloom Stalker’s burst potential.

---

## Umbral Sight

You become invisible to creatures that rely on darkvision.

- While in darkness, you are unseen by such creatures
- You gain darkvision or improved darkvision if you already have it

This allows devastating ambush tactics.

---

## Iron Mind

Your mental defenses strengthen.

- You gain proficiency in Wisdom saving throws
- If you already have it, you gain another saving throw proficiency instead

This improves resilience against mental effects.

---

## Stalker’s Flurry

You capitalize on enemy mistakes.

- Once per turn, when you miss with a weapon attack
- You can make another attack

This feature increases consistency in combat.

---

## Shadowy Dodge

You can evade attacks in darkness.

- When a creature makes an attack roll against you
- You can use your Reaction to impose Disadvantage

This feature reinforces survivability in low-light environments.

---

## Gloom Stalker Philosophy

Gloom Stalkers dominate the shadows.

They excel at:
- Alpha strikes
- Stealth-based combat
- Eliminating priority targets

Their strength lies in ending fights before they truly begin.
