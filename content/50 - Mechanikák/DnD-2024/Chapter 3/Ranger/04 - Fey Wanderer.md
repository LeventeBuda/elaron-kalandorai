
Fey Wanderers are rangers touched by the magic of the Feywild.  
Their presence unsettles enemies and enchants allies, blending martial skill with otherworldly influence.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Dreadful Strikes

You can infuse your attacks with unsettling fey magic.

- Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a weapon attack
- You deal extra psychic damage
- The damage increases as you gain ranger levels

This reflects the lingering influence of the Feywild.

---

## Fey Wanderer Magic

You learn additional spells at certain ranger levels.

| Ranger Level | Spells |
|-------------|--------|
| 3rd | Charm Person |
| 5th | Misty Step |
| 9th | Dispel Magic |
| 13th | Dimension Door |
| 17th | Mislead |

These spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Otherworldly Glamour

Your fey presence enhances your social grace.

- You gain proficiency in one of the following skills: Deception, Performance, or Persuasion
- You add your Wisdom modifier to Charisma checks

This feature allows you to influence others with uncanny charm.

---

## Beguiling Twist

You can turn enchantment and fear against your enemies.

- When a creature succeeds or fails on a saving throw against being charmed or frightened
- You can use your Reaction to redirect the effect to another creature

This feature rewards clever positioning and timing.

---

## Fey Reinforcements

You can summon fey spirits to aid you.

- You can cast *Summon Fey*
- The spell can be cast without concentration a limited number of times

This dramatically increases your battlefield presence.

---

## Misty Wanderer

You can teleport through fey magic.

- You can cast *Misty Step* without expending a spell slot
- After doing so, you can bring willing creatures with you

This enhances mobility and team positioning.

---

## Fey Wanderer Philosophy

Fey Wanderers blend steel and charm.

They excel at:
- Psychological warfare
- Mobility and control
- Social encounters with supernatural flair

Their power comes from embracing the strange beauty of the Feywild.
