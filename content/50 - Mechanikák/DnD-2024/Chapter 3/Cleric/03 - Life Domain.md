
The Life Domain focuses on vitality, restoration, and the preservation of life.  
Clerics of this domain are powerful healers who channel divine energy to mend wounds and protect the living.

---

## Domain Features

You gain the following features when you choose the Life Domain.

---

## Domain Spells

You gain domain spells at the cleric levels listed.

| Cleric Level | Spells |
|--------------|--------|
| 1st | Bless, Cure Wounds |
| 3rd | Aid, Lesser Restoration |
| 5th | Beacon of Hope, Revivify |
| 7th | Death Ward, Guardian of Faith |
| 9th | Mass Cure Wounds, Raise Dead |

Domain spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Bonus Proficiency

When you choose this domain:
- You gain proficiency with heavy armor

This allows you to better protect yourself while tending to others.

---

## Disciple of Life

Your healing spells are especially effective.

- When you use a spell of 1st level or higher to restore hit points
- The creature regains additional hit points

This feature enhances all magical healing you provide.

---

## Channel Divinity: Preserve Life

You can use Channel Divinity to restore life energy.

- As an action, you present your holy symbol
- You restore hit points to creatures you can see
- You cannot restore a creature above half its hit point maximum

This feature allows rapid recovery during combat.

---

## Blessed Healer

Your healing magic restores you as well.

- When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher that restores hit points to another creature
- You regain hit points yourself

This encourages clerics to remain active healers on the battlefield.

---

## Supreme Healer

Your most powerful healing spells become more reliable.

- When you would roll dice to restore hit points with a spell
- You instead use the maximum value for those dice

This feature ensures consistent, potent healing.

---

## Life Domain Philosophy

Clerics of the Life Domain are guardians and nurturers.

They excel at:
- Sustaining allies through prolonged encounters
- Recovering from devastating damage
- Standing as beacons of hope in desperate situations

Their power reflects compassion backed by unwavering divine strength.
