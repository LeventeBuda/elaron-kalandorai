
Clerics are divine spellcasters who serve gods, ideals, or cosmic forces.  
They wield magic granted by faith, channeling divine power to heal allies, smite foes, and shape the battlefield.

---

## Class Identity

Clerics are defined by:
- Divine spellcasting
- Strong support and healing capabilities
- Access to armor and weapons
- Powers granted by a divine domain

They can serve as healers, frontline combatants, or spell-focused supporters depending on their domain.

---

## Primary Ability

**Wisdom** is the cleric’s primary ability score.

- Determines spellcasting effectiveness
- Influences spell save DCs and attack rolls
- Affects perception and insight

**Constitution** is important for durability, especially for frontline clerics.

---

## Hit Points

- **Hit Die:** d8 per cleric level

Clerics balance survivability and spellcasting.

## Core Class Features

---

## Spellcasting

Clerics prepare spells from the cleric spell list.

- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom
- You can change prepared spells after a Long Rest
- You can cast spells as rituals if the spell allows it

Cleric magic focuses on healing, protection, and divine wrath.

---

## Divine Order

Your divine calling shapes your role.

You choose a Divine Order that grants:
- Additional proficiencies
- A thematic role within the faith

This choice reflects how you serve your divine power.

---

## Channel Divinity

You can channel divine energy to fuel special effects.

- Uses are limited and refresh on a Short or Long Rest
- Different domains grant unique Channel Divinity options

---

## Divine Domain

At 3rd level, you choose a **Divine Domain**.

Your domain represents the aspect of divinity you serve and grants features at higher levels.

Available domains include:
- Life
- Light
- Trickery
- War

Each domain is detailed in its own note.

---

## Destroy Undead

Your divine power can obliterate lesser undead.

- Undead that fail their saving throw are destroyed
- The maximum challenge rating increases as you level

---

## Blessed Strikes

Your attacks are infused with divine power.

- Once per turn, you deal extra radiant damage
- Applies to weapon attacks or cantrips

---

## Divine Intervention

You can call directly upon your deity for aid.

- You make a percentile roll
- On success, your deity intervenes
- At higher levels, success becomes guaranteed

This feature represents the pinnacle of divine favor.

---

## Cleric in the Party

Clerics excel as:
- Healers and protectors
- Support spellcasters
- Frontline combatants with the right domain

They are less focused on:
- Stealth
- Pure damage specialization without support
