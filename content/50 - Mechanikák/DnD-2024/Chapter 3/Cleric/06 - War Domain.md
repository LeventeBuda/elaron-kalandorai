
The War Domain represents discipline, battle prowess, and divine command on the battlefield.  
Clerics of this domain serve gods of war, strategy, and conquest, blending martial skill with divine magic.

---

## Domain Features

You gain the following features when you choose the War Domain.

---

## Domain Spells

You gain domain spells at the cleric levels listed.

| Cleric Level | Spells |
|--------------|--------|
| 1st | Divine Favor, Shield of Faith |
| 3rd | Magic Weapon, Spiritual Weapon |
| 5th | Crusader’s Mantle, Spirit Guardians |
| 7th | Freedom of Movement, Stoneskin |
| 9th | Flame Strike, Hold Monster |

Domain spells are always prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare.

---

## Bonus Proficiencies

When you choose this domain:
- You gain proficiency with martial weapons
- You gain proficiency with heavy armor

This allows you to fight alongside frontline warriors.

---

## War Priest

Your god grants you extra attacks in combat.

- When you take the Attack action
- You can make one weapon attack as a Bonus Action

You can use this feature a limited number of times, refreshing on a Long Rest.

---

## Channel Divinity: Guided Strike

You can use Channel Divinity to ensure a successful blow.

- When you make an attack roll
- You can add a significant bonus to the roll after seeing the result

This feature represents divine guidance in battle.

---

## Channel Divinity: War God’s Blessing

You can extend divine guidance to others.

- When a creature you can see makes an attack roll
- You can use your Reaction to grant a bonus to the roll

This allows you to direct the flow of combat beyond your own actions.

---

## Divine Strike

Your weapon attacks are infused with divine power.

- Once per turn, you deal extra weapon damage
- The damage increases at higher levels

This feature reinforces your role as a martial cleric.

---

## Avatar of Battle

At higher levels, divine protection shields you in combat.

- You gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical weapons

This feature represents divine favor in the heart of war.

---

## War Domain Philosophy

Clerics of the War Domain are battlefield leaders.

They excel at:
- Frontline combat
- Supporting allies through divine tactics
- Turning the tide of battle with decisive intervention

Their faith is proven through strength, courage, and victory.
