
Battle Masters are tactical combatants who use training and experience to control the battlefield.  
They employ maneuvers fueled by superiority dice to outthink and outfight their enemies.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Combat Superiority

You learn maneuvers that are powered by **superiority dice**.

- You gain a number of superiority dice
- Superiority dice are expended to fuel maneuvers
- A superiority die is expended when you use it
- You regain all expended superiority dice on a Short or Long Rest

The size and number of superiority dice increase as you gain levels.

---

## Maneuvers

You learn a number of maneuvers of your choice.

- Each maneuver uses one superiority die
- Maneuvers add tactical effects to your attacks
- You can use only one maneuver per attack

Maneuvers represent trained techniques rather than magical abilities.

---

## Saving Throws

Some maneuvers require a saving throw.

- The saving throw DC is based on your Strength or Dexterity
- You choose which ability score to use

## Know Your Enemy

You can analyze opponents in combat.

- After observing or interacting with a creature
- You learn information about its capabilities
- This includes relative Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Armor Class, or Hit Points

This feature rewards preparation and observation.

---

## Improved Combat Superiority

Your combat techniques become more effective.

- Your superiority dice increase in size
- You gain additional maneuvers

This reflects mastery gained through experience.

---

## Relentless

You recover superiority dice in the heat of battle.

- When you roll initiative and have no superiority dice remaining
- You regain one superiority die

This ensures continued tactical options even in prolonged encounters.

---

## Battle Master Philosophy

Battle Masters excel through planning and precision.

They thrive when:
- Positioning and timing matter
- Enemies rely on brute force
- Tactical decisions shape the outcome of combat

Their strength lies in skill, not magic.
