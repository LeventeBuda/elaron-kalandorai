
Psi Warriors augment their martial prowess with psionic power.  
Through mental discipline, they channel psychic energy to protect allies, strike foes, and manipulate the battlefield.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Psionic Power

You harness psionic energy represented by **Psionic Energy dice**.

- You have a number of Psionic Energy dice
- The die size increases as you gain fighter levels
- You regain expended dice after a Long Rest
- You can regain one die by expending a use of Second Wind

These dice fuel your psionic abilities.

---

## Protective Field

You can shield yourself or others with telekinetic force.

- When you or a creature you can see takes damage
- You can use your Reaction and expend a Psionic Energy die
- Reduce the damage by the number rolled plus your Intelligence modifier

This feature enhances party survivability.

---

## Psionic Strike

You can empower your attacks with psychic force.

- When you hit a target with a weapon attack
- You can expend a Psionic Energy die to deal extra psychic damage

This provides reliable bonus damage without spellcasting.

---

## Telekinetic Movement

You can move creatures or objects with your mind.

- As an action, you can move a creature or object you can see
- The target is moved without provoking Opportunity Attacks
- This feature expends a Psionic Energy die

This allows repositioning and creative battlefield control.

---

## Psi-Powered Leap

You can propel yourself with telekinetic force.

- As a Bonus Action, you gain a flying speed for the turn
- You must land by the end of your turn or fall

This grants exceptional mobility.

---

## Telekinetic Thrust

Your psionic attacks can disrupt enemies.

- When you deal damage with Psionic Strike
- You can force the target to make a saving throw
- On a failure, the target is knocked prone or pushed

This adds control to your offense.

---

## Guarded Mind

Your mental defenses harden.

- You gain resistance to psychic damage
- You can end the Charmed or Frightened condition on yourself

This reflects superior mental discipline.

---

## Bulwark of Force

You can project psionic shields to protect allies.

- As a Bonus Action, you grant cover-like protection to nearby creatures
- The effect lasts for a limited duration

This feature emphasizes defensive leadership.

---

## Psi Warrior Philosophy

Psi Warriors blend mind and muscle.

They excel at:
- Battlefield control
- Defensive support
- Consistent psychic-enhanced damage

Their power comes from focus, discipline, and mental strength.
