
Draconic Bloodline sorcerers draw their magic from draconic ancestry.  
Scales, elemental power, and an imposing presence mark those whose blood carries the might of dragons.

---

## Sorcerous Origin Features

You gain the following features when you choose this origin.

---

## Dragon Ancestor

Your lineage traces back to a specific type of dragon.

- Choose a dragon type associated with an elemental damage type
- This choice determines certain class features
- You can speak, read, and write Draconic

Your ancestry shapes the nature of your magic.

---

## Draconic Resilience

Your body reflects draconic toughness.

- Your hit point maximum increases
- When you are not wearing armor, your Armor Class equals **13 + Dexterity modifier**

This provides natural durability uncommon among spellcasters.

---

## Elemental Affinity

Your magic channels draconic energy.

- When you cast a spell that deals your chosen elemental damage
- You add your Charisma modifier to one damage roll
- You can spend Sorcery Points to gain resistance to that damage type

This feature reinforces elemental specialization.

---

## Dragon Wings

You manifest draconic wings.

- As a Bonus Action, you gain a flying speed
- The wings persist until dismissed

This dramatically enhances mobility and battlefield presence.

---

## Draconic Presence

You radiate the terrifying majesty of dragons.

- As an action, you can force nearby creatures to make a saving throw
- On a failure, they become frightened or charmed by you

This feature represents overwhelming draconic authority.

---

## Draconic Bloodline Philosophy

Draconic sorcerers embody raw elemental power.

They excel at:
- Elemental damage
- Personal durability
- Dominating the battlefield through presence

Their strength comes from ancient blood and draconic pride.
