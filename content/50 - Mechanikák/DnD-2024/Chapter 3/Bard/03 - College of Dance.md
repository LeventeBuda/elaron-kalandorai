
Bards of the College of Dance channel magic through movement, rhythm, and physical expression.  
Their performances are fluid and kinetic, blending grace, agility, and spellcasting into a seamless art.

---

## College Features

You gain the following features when you join the College of Dance.

---

## Dazzling Footwork

Your movements are mesmerizing.

- While you are not wearing medium or heavy armor and not using a shield:
  - Your movement becomes more fluid and expressive
  - Your dance enhances your battlefield presence

This feature emphasizes mobility and positioning.

---

## Dance of Battle

You can weave magic into combat through dance.

- You can use Bardic Inspiration to enhance your movement or combat actions
- Your performance turns motion into magical expression

This ability rewards staying active and mobile during encounters.

---

## Graceful Flourish

Your dance becomes a weapon.

- You can enhance attacks or actions with elegant, flowing movements
- These flourishes combine offense, defense, and style

This feature represents mastery of combat-as-performance.

---

## Inspiring Motion

Your movement bolsters your allies.

- Allies who witness your dance gain benefits tied to your Bardic Inspiration
- The effect reinforces teamwork and momentum

---

## Master of the Dance

At higher levels, your body and magic move as one.

- Your dance transcends physical limits
- You maintain magical effects through constant motion

This feature reflects total harmony between spell, body, and rhythm.

---

## College of Dance Philosophy

Bards of the College of Dance thrive in motion.

They excel when:
- Positioning and mobility matter
- Combat is dynamic
- Style and presence shape the battlefield

Their magic is seen as much as it is felt.
