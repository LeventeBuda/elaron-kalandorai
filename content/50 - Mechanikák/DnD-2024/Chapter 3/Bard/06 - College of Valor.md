
Bards of the College of Valor are inspirational warriors.  
They combine martial training with magic and music, leading allies through courage, rhythm, and heroic example.

---

## College Features

You gain the following features when you join the College of Valor.

---

## Bonus Proficiencies

When you choose this college:
- You gain proficiency with medium armor
- You gain proficiency with shields
- You gain proficiency with martial weapons

These proficiencies allow you to stand on the front lines beside other warriors.

---

## Combat Inspiration

You learn to use Bardic Inspiration in the heat of battle.

When a creature has one of your Bardic Inspiration dice, it can:
- Add the die to a weapon damage roll, or
- Add the die to its Armor Class against one attack

The creature can use the die after seeing the roll, but before the outcome is determined.

---

## Extra Attack

Starting at higher levels:
- You can attack twice, instead of once, when you take the Attack action on your turn

This feature brings the bard closer to martial classes in sustained combat.

---

## Battle Magic

You learn to weave magic and steel together.

When you use your Action to cast a bard spell:
- You can make one weapon attack as a Bonus Action

This allows seamless blending of spellcasting and weapon combat.

---

## College of Valor Philosophy

Valor bards inspire through action.

They excel when:
- Allies fight shoulder to shoulder
- Courage and morale decide the battle
- Magic and martial skill must work together

Their songs are anthems of heroism, sung in the clash of steel and spell.
