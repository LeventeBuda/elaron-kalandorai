
Assassins specialize in stealth, disguise, and lethal precision.  
They strike from the shadows, eliminating targets before enemies can react.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Bonus Proficiencies

You gain proficiency with:
- Disguise kits
- Poisoner’s kits

These tools support infiltration and silent elimination.

---

## Assassinate

You excel at catching enemies off guard.

- You have Advantage on attack rolls against creatures that have not yet taken a turn in combat
- Any hit you score against a surprised creature is a critical hit

This feature defines the assassin’s devastating opening strike.

---

## Infiltration Expertise

You can create detailed false identities.

- You can establish a convincing cover identity
- Preparing an identity requires time and resources

This feature supports long-term deception.

---

## Impostor

You can flawlessly mimic others.

- You can replicate speech, writing, and behavior
- Creatures have difficulty detecting your deception

This enables deep infiltration.

---

## Death Strike

Your opening attacks can be instantly lethal.

- When you hit a surprised creature
- The target must make a saving throw or take massive damage

This feature represents the assassin’s ultimate killing technique.

---

## Assassin Philosophy

Assassins excel at preparation and timing.

They thrive when:
- Surprise can be achieved
- High-value targets must be eliminated
- Stealth and deception are central to the mission

Their strength lies in ending threats before they begin.
