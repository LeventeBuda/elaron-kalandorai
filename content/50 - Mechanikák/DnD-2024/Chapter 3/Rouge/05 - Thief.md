
Thieves are masters of speed, agility, and opportunism.  
They excel at climbing, stealing, and exploiting the environment to their advantage.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Fast Hands

You can perform quick actions with remarkable efficiency.

- You can use the Use an Object action as a Bonus Action
- You can also use thieves’ tools quickly

This allows rapid interaction with the environment.

---

## Second-Story Work

You are exceptionally skilled at climbing and jumping.

- You gain a climbing speed
- You increase your jumping distance

This feature enhances mobility in urban environments.

---

## Supreme Sneak

You move silently and unseen.

- You have Advantage on Stealth checks when you move slowly

This supports careful infiltration.

---

## Use Magic Device

You can activate magical items with ease.

- You ignore class, race, and level requirements on magic items

This makes you exceptionally versatile.

---

## Thief’s Reflexes

Your reflexes are unmatched.

- You take two turns during the first round of combat
- The first turn occurs at your normal initiative, the second at initiative minus 10

This feature defines the thief’s explosive opening potential.

---

## Thief Philosophy

Thieves thrive on speed and opportunity.

They excel at:
- Environmental interaction
- Urban exploration
- Rapid tactical decision-making

Their power lies in acting faster than anyone else.
