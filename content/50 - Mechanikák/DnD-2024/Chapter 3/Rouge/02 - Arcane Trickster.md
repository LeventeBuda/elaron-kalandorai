
Arcane Tricksters blend roguish skill with arcane magic.  
They use illusion and enchantment to deceive enemies, control the battlefield, and enhance their natural cunning.

---

## Archetype Features

You gain the following features when you choose this archetype.

---

## Spellcasting

You learn to cast wizard spells.

- Spellcasting ability: Intelligence
- You know a limited number of spells from the wizard spell list
- Most of your spells must be from the illusion or enchantment schools

You gain spell slots and spells known as shown on the Arcane Trickster table.

---

## Mage Hand Legerdemain

Your *Mage Hand* becomes more versatile.

- You can cast *Mage Hand* as a bonus action
- The hand is invisible
- You can use it to manipulate objects, disarm traps, or pick locks at range

This feature defines the Arcane Tricksterâ€™s signature trickery.

---

## Magical Ambush

You exploit distractions to empower your magic.

- When you are hidden from a creature
- That creature has Disadvantage on saving throws against your spells

This greatly improves spell reliability.

---

## Versatile Trickster

You use your magic to gain advantage.

- You can distract enemies using your Mage Hand
- You can gain Advantage on attack rolls against distracted targets

This enhances Sneak Attack consistency.

---

## Spell Thief

You can steal magical knowledge.

- When a creature casts a spell
- You can attempt to interrupt and learn it
- You may cast the stolen spell later

This feature represents supreme arcane misdirection.

---

## Arcane Trickster Philosophy

Arcane Tricksters excel at magical deception.

They thrive when:
- Illusions and enchantments dominate encounters
- Positioning and timing matter
- Creativity is rewarded

Their power lies in clever use of magic rather than raw force.
