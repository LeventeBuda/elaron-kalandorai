
The Inner Planes are realms of raw elemental matter and energy.  
They represent the fundamental building blocks of the multiverse.

---

## Nature of the Inner Planes

The Inner Planes are composed of pure elements.

- Matter and energy exist in extreme forms
- Survival often requires protection or magic
- Natural laws may differ from the Material Plane

These planes are inhospitable to unprepared travelers.

---

## Elemental Planes

The four primary Elemental Planes are:

- **Elemental Plane of Air**
- **Elemental Plane of Earth**
- **Elemental Plane of Fire**
- **Elemental Plane of Water**

Each plane embodies its element completely.

---

## Elemental Plane of Air

A vast sky filled with clouds and winds.

- Gravity is weak or directional
- Floating landmasses and storms are common
- Creatures fly or drift freely

---

## Elemental Plane of Earth

An endless expanse of stone and minerals.

- Movement is slow and difficult
- Caverns and tunnels form natural pathways
- Rich veins of metal and gems exist

---

## Elemental Plane of Fire

A realm of flame and heat.

- Fire dominates the environment
- Most areas are lethal without protection
- Cities of fire-adapted beings may exist

---

## Elemental Plane of Water

A boundless ocean.

- Water extends in all directions
- Pressure and currents are constant hazards
- Islands and air pockets are rare but valuable

---

## Border Elemental Regions

Between elemental planes lie mixed regions.

- Elements blend into new environments
- Conditions are unpredictable
- Unique creatures and phenomena arise

These regions blur elemental boundaries.

---

## The Inner Planes in Play

The Inner Planes are used to:
- Showcase extreme environments
- Introduce elemental threats or allies
- Explain the origin of elemental creatures

They are places of raw power and danger.
