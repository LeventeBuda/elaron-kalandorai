
Transitive planes are realms that connect other planes of existence.  
They function as pathways, boundaries, or mediums through which travel between planes is possible.

---

## Role of Transitive Planes

Transitive planes:
- Link the Material Plane to other planes
- Enable magical and planar travel
- Often overlay or border multiple planes at once

They are rarely destinations, but often necessary routes.

---

## Common Transitive Planes

The most well-known transitive planes include:
- The Astral Plane
- The Ethereal Plane
- The Shadowfell

Each serves a distinct purpose in planar movement.

---

## The Ethereal Plane

The Ethereal Plane overlaps the Material Plane.

- Creatures can see the Material Plane from the Ethereal
- Movement through solid objects is possible
- Ethereal travel allows bypassing physical barriers

It is commonly accessed through magic.

---

## The Shadowfell

The Shadowfell is a dark reflection of the Material Plane.

- Landscapes are familiar but bleak
- Emotions are muted or oppressive
- It is associated with death and decay

Travel through the Shadowfell can be dangerous and disorienting.

---

## Planar Boundaries

Transitive planes often form boundaries.

- Crossing them may require magic or portals
- Certain locations are naturally thin between planes
- Planar rifts may open unexpectedly

Such areas are often sites of adventure.

---

## Dangers of Transitive Planes

Transitive planes pose unique risks.

- Navigation can be confusing
- Hostile entities may patrol boundaries
- Prolonged exposure may affect mind or body

Travelers should prepare carefully.

---

## Transitive Planes in Play

Transitive planes are used to:
- Explain planar movement
- Enable dramatic transitions
- Introduce strange encounters

They blur the line between worlds.
