
General Feats represent broad training, talent, or experience.  
Most characters can choose from these feats when they gain a feat option.

Unless stated otherwise, a feat can be taken only once.

---

## Actor

You are skilled at mimicry and dramatic performance.

- Increase Charisma
- You have advantage on Deception and Performance checks when pretending to be someone else
- You can mimic speech and sounds you have heard

---

## Athlete

You have trained your body to peak physical performance.

- Increase Strength or Dexterity
- Climbing no longer costs extra movement
- You can make standing jumps more easily

---

## Charger

You excel at aggressive movement in combat.

- When you Dash, you can make a powerful melee attack
- The attack deals extra damage or pushes the target

---

## Defensive Duelist

You are adept at parrying attacks.

- When wielding a finesse weapon
- You can use your Reaction to increase your Armor Class
- The bonus scales with proficiency

---

## Dual Wielder

You master fighting with two weapons.

- You can wield two non-light weapons
- You gain a bonus to Armor Class while dual wielding
- You can draw or stow two weapons at once

---

## Durable

Your body recovers quickly from injury.

- Increase Constitution
- You regain more hit points when spending Hit Dice

---

## Elemental Adept

You specialize in a particular damage type.

- Choose one elemental damage type
- Spells you cast ignore resistance to that damage
- Damage rolls treat low results as higher

---

## Grappler

You are skilled at wrestling foes.

- You have advantage on attack rolls against creatures you are grappling
- You can restrain a grappled creature more easily

---

## Mage Slayer

You are trained to combat spellcasters.

- You can strike casters when they cast spells nearby
- Spellcasters have disadvantage on concentration saves against your attacks

---

## Observant

You notice what others miss.

- Increase Intelligence or Wisdom
- You gain bonuses to passive Perception and Investigation
- You can read lips with ease

---

## Resilient

You strengthen a particular saving throw.

- Increase one ability score
- You gain proficiency in saving throws using that ability

---

## Ritual Caster

You have learned to cast ritual magic.

- Choose a spellcasting class
- You gain a ritual spellbook
- You can cast ritual spells without spell slots

---

## Sharpshooter

You are deadly at range.

- Your ranged attacks ignore cover
- You can take a penalty to hit for extra damage

---

## Spell Sniper

You extend the reach of your magic.

- Double the range of spells that require attack rolls
- Ignore partial cover with spell attacks
- Learn one cantrip that requires an attack roll

---

## Tavern Brawler

You thrive in rough-and-tumble fights.

- Increase Strength or Constitution
- You are proficient with improvised weapons
- You can grapple as a bonus action

---

## War Caster

You maintain spellcasting under pressure.

- You have advantage on Constitution saves to maintain concentration
- You can cast spells as opportunity attacks
- You can perform somatic components while holding weapons or shields

---
