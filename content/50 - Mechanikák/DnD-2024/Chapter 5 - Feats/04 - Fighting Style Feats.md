
Fighting Style Feats represent disciplined martial training focused on specific combat techniques.  
These feats are typically chosen by characters with formal battle experience.

Unless stated otherwise, a Fighting Style Feat can be taken only once.

---

## Archery

You specialize in ranged weapon combat.

- You gain a bonus to attack rolls with ranged weapons
- Your precision improves your effectiveness at long range

---

## Blind Fighting

You fight effectively without relying on sight.

- You gain blindsight within a short range
- You can perceive invisible or hidden creatures nearby

---

## Defense

You focus on personal protection.

- While wearing armor, you gain a bonus to Armor Class

---

## Dueling

You master fighting with a single weapon.

- When wielding a melee weapon in one hand
- You deal extra damage with that weapon

---

## Great Weapon Fighting

You specialize in heavy melee weapons.

- When you roll low damage dice with a two-handed weapon
- You can reroll the damage dice

---

## Interception

You protect allies from harm.

- When a creature you can see attacks an ally nearby
- You can use your Reaction to reduce the damage

---

## Protection

You shield allies with tactical positioning.

- When a creature attacks a target other than you
- You can impose disadvantage on the attack roll

---

## Thrown Weapon Fighting

You are skilled with thrown weapons.

- You can draw a thrown weapon as part of the attack
- You deal extra damage with thrown weapon attacks

---

## Two-Weapon Fighting

You master fighting with a weapon in each hand.

- You can add your ability modifier to the damage of off-hand attacks

---

## Unarmed Fighting

You specialize in close-quarters combat.

- Your unarmed strikes deal increased damage
- You deal extra damage when grappling creatures

---
