
Origin Feats are gained at 1st level through a character’s background.  
They represent early training, natural talent, or formative life experience.

Unless stated otherwise, an Origin Feat can be taken only once.

---

## Alert

You are always on the lookout for danger.

- You gain a bonus to Initiative
- You can’t be surprised while conscious
- Hidden creatures don’t gain advantage on attack rolls against you

---

## Crafter

You are skilled at creating and repairing objects.

- You gain proficiency with artisan’s tools
- Crafting mundane items takes less time
- You can repair damaged nonmagical objects more efficiently

---

## Healer

You know how to treat wounds quickly and effectively.

- You can restore hit points using a healer’s kit
- You can stabilize creatures without expending magic
- Your healing is especially effective at low levels

---

## Lucky

Fortune favors you.

- You gain a limited number of luck points
- You can spend a luck point to reroll a d20
- You can choose which roll to use

Luck points refresh after a Long Rest.

---

## Magic Initiate (Arcane)

You learn minor arcane magic.

- You learn two cantrips from the wizard spell list
- You learn one 1st-level spell from the same list
- You can cast the spell once without a slot per Long Rest

Your spellcasting ability is Intelligence.

---

## Magic Initiate (Divine)

You learn minor divine magic.

- You learn two cantrips from the cleric spell list
- You learn one 1st-level spell from the same list
- You can cast the spell once without a slot per Long Rest

Your spellcasting ability is Wisdom or Charisma (choose when you gain this feat).

---

## Magic Initiate (Primal)

You learn primal magic tied to nature.

- You learn two cantrips from the druid spell list
- You learn one 1st-level spell from the same list
- You can cast the spell once without a slot per Long Rest

Your spellcasting ability is Wisdom.

---

## Musician

You inspire others through performance.

- You gain proficiency with musical instruments
- During a Short Rest, you can inspire allies
- Inspired allies gain a small bonus on certain rolls

---

## Savage Attacker

You fight with brutal efficiency.

- Once per turn when you deal weapon damage
- You can reroll the damage dice
- You must use the new result

---

## Sentinel

You excel at controlling enemies on the battlefield.

- Creatures provoke opportunity attacks even when disengaging
- You can stop a creature’s movement on a hit
- You can react to attacks against nearby allies

---

## Skilled

You gain broad practical training.

- You gain proficiency in three skills of your choice
- Alternatively, you may choose tool proficiencies

---

## Tough

You are exceptionally hardy.

- Your hit point maximum increases
- You gain additional hit points each time you gain a level

---
