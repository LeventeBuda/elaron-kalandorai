
Epic Boon Feats represent legendary power gained by characters who have surpassed mortal limits.  
These feats are typically granted at very high levels or as special rewards.

Epic Boons are not chosen lightly and are usually awarded by the DM.

---

## Boon of Combat Prowess

Your mastery of battle is unmatched.

- You gain a bonus to attack rolls
- You deal additional damage with weapon attacks
- Your strikes are exceptionally reliable

---

## Boon of Dimensional Travel

You move freely through space.

- You can teleport short distances at will
- You can bypass obstacles and restraints
- Your movement ignores difficult terrain caused by magic

---

## Boon of Energy Resistance

You are shielded from destructive forces.

- Choose one damage type
- You gain resistance to that damage
- In extreme cases, damage may be reduced further

---

## Boon of Fortitude

Your body is extraordinarily resilient.

- Your hit point maximum increases significantly
- You regain hit points more efficiently
- You resist effects that would weaken or exhaust you

---

## Boon of High Magic

Your spellcasting reaches mythic levels.

- You gain an additional high-level spell slot
- You can cast powerful spells with greater frequency
- Your magical endurance improves dramatically

---

## Boon of Irresistible Offense

Your attacks are devastating.

- Your attacks ignore resistance to damage
- Even immune creatures may suffer partial damage
- Your offense cannot easily be negated

---

## Boon of Peerless Aim

Your accuracy borders on supernatural.

- You gain a significant bonus to attack rolls
- You rarely miss, even under adverse conditions

---

## Boon of Recovery

Your ability to recover from harm is extraordinary.

- You regain hit points automatically over time
- You recover quickly from severe injuries
- Defeat is rarely permanent for you

---

## Boon of Skill Proficiency

You achieve perfection in your talents.

- You gain proficiency in all skills
- If already proficient, you gain expertise instead

---

## Boon of Speed

You move faster than thought.

- Your speed increases dramatically
- You gain additional movement options
- You can reposition effortlessly in combat

---
