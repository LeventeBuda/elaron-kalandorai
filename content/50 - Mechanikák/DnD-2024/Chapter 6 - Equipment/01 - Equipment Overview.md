
Equipment includes all the gear a character uses to survive, fight, and interact with the world.  
Weapons, armor, tools, and supplies define what a character can carry, wield, and rely on during adventures.

---

## Acquiring Equipment

Characters can acquire equipment in several ways:
- Purchasing items with coin
- Receiving starting equipment from class or background
- Finding or looting items during adventures
- Crafting items using tools and materials

Unless stated otherwise, mundane equipment is widely available in settlements.

---

## Buying and Selling

- Equipment can usually be purchased at listed prices
- Selling mundane items typically yields half their listed value
- Availability and pricing may vary by location at the DM’s discretion

Rare or specialized items may require contacts or special effort to obtain.

---

## Currency

The standard coins used for trade are:
- Copper pieces (cp)
- Silver pieces (sp)
- Electrum pieces (ep)
- Gold pieces (gp)
- Platinum pieces (pp)

Gold pieces are the most commonly used unit of value.

---

## Carrying Capacity

A character’s ability to carry equipment is limited.

- Carrying capacity is based on Strength
- Exceeding this limit can slow movement or impose penalties
- Some creatures or features modify carrying capacity

The DM determines the effects of encumbrance, if used.

---

## Equipment Proficiency

Some equipment requires training.

- Weapons and armor require proficiency to use effectively
- Using equipment without proficiency can impose disadvantages or limitations
- Tool proficiency allows effective use and crafting

Proficiencies are gained from class, background, or species.

---

## Using Equipment

Most equipment is used through actions.

- Weapons are used with the Attack action
- Some items require the Utilize action
- Certain items function automatically or passively

Each item specifies how it is used.

---

## Damage and Equipment

Equipment interacts with damage in several ways:
- Weapons deal specific damage types
- Armor increases Armor Class
- Shields provide defensive bonuses

Equipment condition and damage are adjudicated by the DM.

---

## Equipment and the World

Equipment reflects culture, technology, and environment.

- Items may look or function differently across regions
- Availability can shape character choices
- Equipment often tells a story about its owner
