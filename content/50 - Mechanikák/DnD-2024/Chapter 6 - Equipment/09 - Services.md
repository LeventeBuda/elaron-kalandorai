
Services represent labor, hospitality, and assistance that characters can purchase with coin.  
These services support travel, downtime, and everyday life in settlements.

---

## Food, Drink, and Lodging

Characters can pay for:
- Meals and beverages
- Rooms at inns or lodgings
- Long-term accommodations

Quality and price depend on location and lifestyle.

---

## Travel Services

Travel often requires hired help.

Examples include:
- Guides
- Porters
- Animal handlers
- Carriage drivers

Hiring skilled assistance can reduce risk and travel time.

---

## Hirelings

Hirelings are NPCs employed for specific tasks.

Types of hirelings include:
- Skilled hirelings (craftspeople, experts)
- Unskilled hirelings (laborers, porters)

Hirelings expect fair pay and reasonable treatment.

---

## Spellcasting Services

In some settlements, spellcasting can be purchased.

Examples include:
- Healing magic
- Divination
- Curse removal

Availability depends on the presence of capable spellcasters.

---

## Other Services

Additional services may include:
- Equipment maintenance
- Item identification
- Training or instruction
- Message delivery

The DM determines cost and availability.

---

## Services in Play

Services help ground the game world.

- They encourage interaction with settlements
- They create opportunities for roleplay
- They support long-term campaigns

Services reflect the economy and culture of a region.
