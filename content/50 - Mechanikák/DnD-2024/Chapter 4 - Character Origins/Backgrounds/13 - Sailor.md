## Sailor Background Features

### Ship’s Passage

Your experience grants you:
- Free or discounted passage on ships
- Familiarity with shipboard hierarchy and etiquette
- Knowledge of ports, harbors, and sea routes

Captains and crews recognize your value.

---

## Roleplaying a Sailor

Sailors are often:
- Superstitious and pragmatic
- Loyal to crewmates
- Comfortable with danger and uncertainty

They may adventure to escape the sea, chase freedom, or answer the call of distant horizons.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Sailor:
- What kind of ship did you serve on?
- Do you love the sea—or fear it?
- What port feels like home?
