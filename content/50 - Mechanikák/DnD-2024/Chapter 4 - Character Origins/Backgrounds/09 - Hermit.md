### Starting Equipment

You start with the following equipment:
- An herbalism kit  
- A scroll case filled with notes from your studies

## Hermit Background Features

### Discovery

During your isolation, you made a significant discovery.

- The discovery may be spiritual, natural, or arcane
- It could be a lost truth, a hidden place, or forbidden knowledge
- The exact nature of the discovery is determined with the DM

This revelation often drives you to rejoin the world.

---

## Roleplaying a Hermit

Hermits are often:
- Thoughtful and introspective
- Detached from social norms
- Quietly intense or spiritually driven

They may adventure to protect a secret, spread truth, or understand their discovery.

---
