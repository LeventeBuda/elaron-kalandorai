## Scribe Background Features

### Scholarly Access

Your profession allows you to:
- Read and write complex scripts and records
- Navigate libraries, archives, and bureaucratic offices
- Gain access to written records that others struggle to obtain

Officials and scholars often trust your work.

---

## Roleplaying a Scribe

Scribes are often:
- Meticulous and observant
- Curious or cautious
- Deeply respectful of knowledge

They may adventure to preserve lost lore, uncover hidden truths, or escape the confines of bureaucracy.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Scribe:
- What texts did you copy or study?
- Who relies on your records?
- Is knowledge a duty—or a temptation?
