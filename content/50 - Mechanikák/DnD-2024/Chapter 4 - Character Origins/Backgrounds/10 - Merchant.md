## Merchant Background Features

### Commercial Network

Your experience grants you:
- Familiarity with trade routes and markets
- Contacts among merchants, caravan masters, and factors
- The ability to acquire common goods quickly and at fair prices

Your reputation can open doors in commercial hubs.

---

## Roleplaying a Merchant

Merchants are often:
- Pragmatic and calculating
- Well-connected
- Motivated by profit, influence, or opportunity

They may adventure to expand trade routes, recover lost cargo, or secure exclusive deals.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Merchant:
- What goods did you specialize in?
- Who do you owe—or who owes you?
- Is profit your goal, or merely a means to an end?
