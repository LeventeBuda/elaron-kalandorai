## Acolyte Background Features

### Sacred Knowledge

Your familiarity with religious practice allows you to:
- Recall information about deities, rites, and holy traditions
- Identify religious symbols and customs
- Navigate temples and sacred institutions with ease

This knowledge often grants you respect or recognition among the faithful.

---

## Roleplaying an Acolyte

Acolytes are often:
- Devout believers
- Scholars of faith
- Servants of a greater power

Their outlook may be humble, zealous, questioning, or quietly resolute.
