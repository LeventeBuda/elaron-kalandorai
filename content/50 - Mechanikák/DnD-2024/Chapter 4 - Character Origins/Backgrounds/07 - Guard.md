## Guard Background Features

### Watchful Presence

Your experience allows you to:
- Maintain alertness during long watches
- Identify suspicious behavior in crowds
- Navigate guard posts, watch rotations, and command structures

Other guards and officials often recognize your authority.

---

## Roleplaying a Guard

Guards are often:
- Disciplined and dutiful
- Protective of others
- Respectful of rules—or hardened by enforcing them

They may adventure after losing their post, seeking justice, or answering a higher calling.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Guard:
- Who or what did you protect?
- Did you serve honorably—or compromise your ideals?
- Do you still see yourself as law enforcement?
