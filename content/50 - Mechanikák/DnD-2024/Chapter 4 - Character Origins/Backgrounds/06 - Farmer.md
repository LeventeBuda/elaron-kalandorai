## Farmer Background Features

### Agricultural Knowledge

Your experience allows you to:
- Identify crops, livestock, and common farming practices
- Predict weather patterns and seasonal changes
- Find food and shelter in rural environments

Farmers are often welcomed in agrarian communities.

---

## Roleplaying a Farmer

Farmers are often:
- Practical and resilient
- Grounded and community-minded
- Slow to anger, but steady under pressure

They may adventure to protect their homeland, escape hardship, or seek a better future.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Farmer:
- What did you grow or raise?
- What forced you to leave the land?
- Do you long to return home someday?
