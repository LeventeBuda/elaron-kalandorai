## Guide Background Features

### Trailblazer

Your experience allows you to:
- Find safe routes through wilderness and hostile terrain
- Avoid natural hazards and environmental dangers
- Navigate without getting lost in familiar regions

Groups traveling with you move more efficiently and safely.

---

## Roleplaying a Guide

Guides are often:
- Observant and patient
- Pragmatic and cautious
- Comfortable far from civilization

They may adventure to chart unknown lands, protect travelers, or uncover lost paths.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Guide:
- What lands do you know best?
- Who did you guide—and did they survive?
- Do you prefer solitude or responsibility?
