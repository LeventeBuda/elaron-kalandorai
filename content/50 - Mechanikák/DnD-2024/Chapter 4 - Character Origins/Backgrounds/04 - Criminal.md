## Criminal Background Features

### Criminal Contacts

You have reliable contacts within criminal networks.

- You know how to send and receive illicit messages
- You can find safe houses, fences, or smugglers
- You understand the hierarchy of local underworld groups

These connections can provide aid—but often at a price.

---

## Roleplaying a Criminal

Criminals are often:
- Street-smart and cautious
- Loyal to trusted allies
- Distrustful of authority

They may adventure to escape their past, expand their influence, or seek redemption.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Criminal:
- What crime defined your reputation?
- Are you still involved—or trying to leave that life behind?
- Who knows your real name?
