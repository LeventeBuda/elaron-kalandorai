## Noble Background Features

### Position of Privilege

Your noble birth grants you influence.

- You are recognized among the upper classes
- Commoners often defer to you
- You can gain audiences with nobles and officials more easily

Your status can open doors—or paint a target on your back.

---

## Roleplaying a Noble

Nobles are often:
- Confident and commanding
- Educated and cultured
- Accustomed to responsibility—or indulgence

They may adventure to prove themselves, escape expectations, or protect family interests.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Noble:
- What is your family’s reputation?
- Do you embrace or resent your status?
- What obligations follow you into adventure?
