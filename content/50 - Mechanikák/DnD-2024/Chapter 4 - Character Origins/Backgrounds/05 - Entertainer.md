## Entertainer Background Features

### By Popular Demand

You can always find a place to perform.

- You receive free lodging and food in exchange for entertainment
- Common folk readily recognize your talent
- You can gather rumors and information easily through audiences

Your reputation opens doors wherever people gather.

---

## Roleplaying an Entertainer

Entertainers are often:
- Charismatic and expressive
- Free-spirited or dramatic
- Comfortable in the spotlight

They may adventure for fame, artistic inspiration, or to bring joy to dark places.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying an Entertainer:
- What form does your performance take?
- Do you crave applause—or connection?
- Is the stage your home, or just a stepping stone?
