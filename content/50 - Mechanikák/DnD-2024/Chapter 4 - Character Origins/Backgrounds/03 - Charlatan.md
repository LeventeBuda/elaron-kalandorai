## Charlatan Background Features

### False Identity

You maintain at least one false identity.

- You have documentation, contacts, and a believable history
- Common people accept your identity at face value
- Maintaining the identity requires care and consistency

This identity can provide shelter, cover, or access to restricted spaces.

---

## Roleplaying a Charlatan

Charlatans are often:
- Clever and adaptable
- Opportunistic
- Comfortable bending the truth

They may adventure for profit, thrill, or to escape a past scheme gone wrong.
