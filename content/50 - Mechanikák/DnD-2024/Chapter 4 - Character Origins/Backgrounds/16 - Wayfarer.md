
You lived a life of constant travel.  
Roads, borders, and unfamiliar cultures were your home long before adventuring began.

---

## Background Benefits

### Ability Scores

- Increase **Dexterity** or **Wisdom**
- Increase one other ability score of your choice

---

### Skill Proficiencies

- Survival  
- Persuasion  

---

### Tool Proficiency

- None

---

### Languages

- One language of your choice

---

### Origin Feat

**Lucky**

Your travels taught you that fortune favors the bold.

---

### Starting Equipment

You start with the following equipment:
- A traveler’s outfit  
- A walking stick  
- A map or collection of travel notes  
- A pouch containing gold  

---

## Wayfarer Background Features

### Seasoned Traveler

Your experience allows you to:
- Navigate roads, borders, and trade routes with ease
- Find shelter and food in unfamiliar regions
- Blend into crowds and new communities

You are rarely truly lost.

---

## Roleplaying a Wayfarer

Wayfarers are often:
- Curious and adaptable
- Restless and independent
- Comfortable among strangers

They may adventure for discovery, escape, or the thrill of the unknown.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Wayfarer:
- What roads have you walked?
- Where do you feel most at home?
- Are you searching for something—or running from it?
