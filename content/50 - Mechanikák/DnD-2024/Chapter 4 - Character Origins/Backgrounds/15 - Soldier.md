## Roleplaying a Soldier

Soldiers are often:
- Disciplined and decisive
- Loyal to comrades
- Marked by past battles

They may adventure after a war ends, desertion, or a personal oath.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Soldier:
- Which army did you serve?
- Do you still follow orders instinctively?
- What battle changed you forever?
