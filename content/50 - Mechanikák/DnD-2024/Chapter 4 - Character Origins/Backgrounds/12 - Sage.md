## Sage Background Features

### Researcher

Your academic training allows you to:
- Recall obscure lore and historical facts
- Know where to find information when you don’t already have it
- Identify institutions, libraries, or experts relevant to a topic

If you don’t know an answer, you usually know where to look.

---

## Roleplaying a Sage

Sages are often:
- Curious and analytical
- Absent-minded or obsessive
- Driven by unanswered questions

They may adventure to recover lost knowledge, test theories, or uncover forbidden truths.

---

## Suggested Characteristics

Consider the following when roleplaying a Sage:
- What field do you study?
- What mystery drives you forward?
- Is knowledge your goal—or power?
