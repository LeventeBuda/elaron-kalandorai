
Dwarves are resilient and tradition-bound folk known for their endurance, craftsmanship, and loyalty.  
Their lives are shaped by stone, clan, and an unyielding sense of honor.

---

## Species Traits

---

### Creature Type

- Humanoid

---

### Size

- Medium

---

### Speed

- 25 feet

---

### Darkvision

Accustomed to life underground, you can see in darkness.

- You have Darkvision out to a limited range

---

### Dwarven Resilience

Your hardy constitution grants you:
- Advantage on saving throws against poison
- Resistance to poison damage

---

### Dwarven Toughness

Your hit point maximum increases.

- You gain additional hit points at 1st level
- You gain additional hit points whenever you gain a level

---

### Stonecunning

You have an instinctive sense for stonework.

- You gain expertise when making History checks related to stone structures

---

## Dwarf Names

Dwarf names are often drawn from ancient clan traditions and carry deep meaning.

---

## Playing a Dwarf

Dwarves are often:
- Stubborn but reliable
- Proud of their ancestry
- Deeply loyal to clan and companions

They endure where others falter.
