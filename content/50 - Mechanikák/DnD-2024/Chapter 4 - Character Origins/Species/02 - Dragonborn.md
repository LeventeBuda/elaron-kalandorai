
Dragonborn are proud draconic humanoids whose ancestry traces back to mighty dragons.  
Their presence carries the echo of ancient power, expressed through breath and resilience.

---

## Species Traits

---

### Creature Type

- Humanoid

---

### Size

- Medium

---

### Speed

- 30 feet

---

### Draconic Ancestry

You have a draconic ancestor that determines your damage type.

Choose one ancestry:
- Acid
- Cold
- Fire
- Lightning
- Poison

This choice affects your Breath Weapon and damage resistance.

---

### Breath Weapon

You can exhale destructive energy.

- As an action, you unleash a breath attack
- Creatures in the area make a saving throw
- On a failure, they take damage based on your ancestry
- On a success, they take half damage
- You can use this trait a limited number of times
- Uses refresh after a Long Rest

---

### Damage Resistance

You have resistance to the damage type associated with your draconic ancestry.

---

### Draconic Presence

Your lineage grants you an imposing demeanor.

- You gain proficiency in either **Intimidation** or **Persuasion**

---

## Dragonborn Names

Dragonborn names often reflect their clan heritage or draconic legacy.  
They may sound harsh, formal, or ancient.

---

## Playing a Dragonborn

Dragonborn are often:
- Proud and honor-bound
- Conscious of legacy and reputation
- Driven to prove their worth

Their draconic blood is both a gift and a responsibility.
