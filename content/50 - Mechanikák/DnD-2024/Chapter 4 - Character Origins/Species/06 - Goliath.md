
Goliaths are towering, mountain-born folk shaped by harsh climates and relentless challenges.  
They value strength, endurance, and proving one’s worth through deeds.

---

## Species Traits

---

### Creature Type

- Humanoid

---

### Size

- Medium

---

### Speed

- 30 feet

---

### Mountain Born

You are adapted to high altitudes and cold climates.

- You are acclimated to high altitude
- You have resistance to cold damage

---

### Powerful Build

You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.

---

### Stone’s Endurance

You can shrug off blows through sheer toughness.

- When you take damage, you can use your Reaction to reduce that damage
- You can use this trait a limited number of times
- Uses refresh after a Long Rest

---

### Athletics Training

Your culture prizes physical prowess.

- You gain proficiency in **Athletics**

---

## Goliath Names

Goliath names are often short, strong-sounding, and earned through achievement.

---

## Playing a Goliath

Goliaths are often:
- Competitive and driven
- Proud of physical achievement
- Respectful of strength in all its forms

Life is a challenge—and they intend to meet it head-on.
