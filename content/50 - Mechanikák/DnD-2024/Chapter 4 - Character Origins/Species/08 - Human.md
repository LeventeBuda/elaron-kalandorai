
Humans are the most adaptable and ambitious of the common peoples.  
Their drive, versatility, and determination allow them to thrive in almost any environment.

### Resourceful

Human adaptability grants you additional versatility.

- You gain proficiency in one skill of your choice
- You gain proficiency in one tool of your choice

---

### Versatile Mind

Your broad experience sharpens your learning ability.

- You gain one additional language of your choice

---

## Human Names

Human names vary widely, reflecting the cultures and regions they come from.

## Playing a Human

Humans are often:
- Driven and ambitious
- Curious and adaptable
- Defined by personal choice rather than destiny

Their greatest strength is their flexibility.
