
Orcs are powerful, relentless warriors shaped by a culture that prizes strength, endurance, and survival.  
They face the world head-on, driven by instinct, willpower, and fierce determination.

### Darkvision

Accustomed to life in harsh and shadowed lands, you can see in darkness.

- You have Darkvision out to a limited range

---

### Adrenaline Rush

You can push your body beyond its normal limits.

- As a Bonus Action, you can take the Dash action
- You gain temporary hit points
- You can use this trait a limited number of times
- Uses refresh after a Long Rest
