### Darkvision

Your infernal heritage grants you vision in darkness.

- You have Darkvision out to a limited range

---

### Infernal Legacy

You know infernal magic tied to your bloodline.

You know the following spells:
- Thaumaturgy (cantrip)

At higher levels, you gain additional spells such as:
- Hellish Rebuke
- Darkness

Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

---

### Fiendish Resistance

You have resistance to:
- Fire damage

---

## Tiefling Names

Tiefling names often reflect infernal heritage, defiance, or adopted cultural identities.

---

## Playing a Tiefling

Tieflings are often:
- Charismatic or guarded
- Defined by choice rather than origin
- Forced to confront prejudice or expectation

Their power is both curse and strength.
