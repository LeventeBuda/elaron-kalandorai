
Gnomes are curious, clever folk driven by invention, illusion, and boundless curiosity.  
Their small stature hides keen intellect and an irrepressible love of discovery.

---

## Species Traits

---

### Creature Type

- Humanoid

---

### Size

- Small

---

### Speed

- 30 feet

---

### Darkvision

Used to life underground and nocturnal tinkering, you can see in darkness.

- You have Darkvision out to a limited range

---

### Gnome Cunning

Your mind is exceptionally resilient.

- You have Advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws against magic

---

### Tinker

You are skilled at crafting small devices.

- You can create simple mechanical objects
- These devices have limited, flavorful effects

---

## Gnome Names

Gnome names are playful, intricate, and often whimsical.

---

## Playing a Gnome

Gnomes are often:
- Inquisitive and inventive
- Optimistic and eccentric
- Driven by curiosity rather than ambition

They find wonder where others see routine.
