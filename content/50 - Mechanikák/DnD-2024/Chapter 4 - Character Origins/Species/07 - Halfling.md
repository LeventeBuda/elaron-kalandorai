
Halflings are small folk known for their optimism, courage, and remarkable luck.  
Though unassuming, they possess resilience and bravery that often surprises larger folk.

---

## Species Traits

---

### Creature Type

- Humanoid

---

### Size

- Small

---

### Speed

- 30 feet

---

### Lucky

Fortune seems to smile upon you.

- When you roll a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw
- You can reroll the die and must use the new result

---

### Brave

Your courage is legendary.

- You have Advantage on saving throws against being frightened

---

### Halfling Nimbleness

You can move through the space of any creature that is larger than you.

---

## Halfling Names

Halfling names are often simple, warm, and rooted in family tradition.

---

## Playing a Halfling

Halflings are often:
- Cheerful and resilient
- Loyal to friends and family
- Brave when it matters most

They prove that heroism comes in all sizes.
