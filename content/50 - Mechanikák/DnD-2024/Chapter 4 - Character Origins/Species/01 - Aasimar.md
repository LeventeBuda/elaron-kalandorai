
Aasimars are mortals touched by celestial power.  
Their souls carry a spark of the Upper Planes, marking them as champions, messengers, or reluctant heirs of divine legacy.

---

## Species Traits

---

### Creature Type

- Humanoid

---

### Size

- Medium

---

### Speed

- 30 feet

---

### Celestial Legacy

You possess divine power inherited from a celestial ancestor or blessing.

You know one of the following cantrips:
- Light
- Sacred Flame

Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell (choose when you gain this trait).

---

### Celestial Resistance

You have resistance to:
- Radiant damage
- Necrotic damage

---

### Healing Hands

You can channel divine energy to heal wounds.

- As an action, you touch a creature
- The creature regains hit points
- You can use this trait a limited number of times
- Uses refresh after a Long Rest

---

### Radiant Revelation

You can unleash your celestial power.

- As a Bonus Action, you transform briefly
- You shed radiant light
- Once per turn, you deal extra radiant damage
- The transformation lasts for a short duration
- You regain the ability to use this trait after a Long Rest

---

## Aasimar Names

Aasimars often bear names inspired by celestial heritage or the culture they were raised in.
